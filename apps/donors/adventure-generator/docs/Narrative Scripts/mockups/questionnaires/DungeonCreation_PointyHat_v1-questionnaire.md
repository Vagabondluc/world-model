# UI Questionnaire: Pointy Hat Dungeon Architect

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


## 1. Primary Purpose
A thematic dungeon design suite based on the 'Pointy Hat' method, focusing on a singular one-word theme that drives game mechanics, objectives, and encounter design for a cohesive Player Experience.

## 2. Core Inputs
- **One-Word Theme:** (Essential: Setting the mood, e.g., 'Rust', 'Echoes', 'Rot').
- **Thematic Mechanics:** (How the world rules reinforce the theme).
- **Core Goal:** (The primary driver for the players).
- **Encounter Seeds:** (The thematic heartbeat of the dungeon).

## 3. UI Requirements
- **Theme Anchor:** A prominent, centralized input for the "One-Word Theme" which remains visible throughout the creation process.
- **Thematic Bridge Editor:** A specialized input area to link "Theme" to "Mechanics" (e.g., Theme: 'Cold' -> Mechanic: 'Exhaustion at night').
- **Encounters & Ending Studio:** A sectionalized editor for designing thematic combat, puzzles, and the final climactic synthesis.
- **Visual Moodboard:** (Optional) A drag-and-drop area for images or color palettes that match the One-Word Theme.
- **Instructional Overlay:** Toggleable tooltips that guide the GM through the specific Step I-V framework.

## 4. Derived & Automated Fields
- **Thematic Keyword Suggester:** Generates associated words and mechanics based on the One-Word Theme.
- **Mechanical Consistency Checker:** Flags encounters that don't visibly reflect the "Mechanics" or "Theme" defined in Step II.
- **Pacing Visualizer:** Shows the progression from Step I (Theme) to Step V (Ending) to ensure narrative tension.

## 5. Exports & Integration
- Pointy Hat Dungeon Module (Markdown).
- Thematic Cheat Sheet for the FM.
- VTT Mechanics manifest.
