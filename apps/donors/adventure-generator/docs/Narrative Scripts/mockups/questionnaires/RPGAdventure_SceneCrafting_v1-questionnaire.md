**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


## 1. Primary Purpose
A storyboard-driven adventure architect that links multiple real-time scenes into a cohesive 6-stage narrative arc.

## 2. Core Inputs
- **Overarching Storyline:** The central mystery or goal.
- **Faction Dynamics:** Who holds sway in this environment?
- **Stage 1 (Prep):** Inciting incident + world intro.
- **Stage 2 (Trials):** Physical and metaphysical challenges.
- **Stage 3 (Midpoint):** The shocking twist or setback.
- **Stage 4 (Intellect/RP):** Puzzles and emotional character moments.
- **Stage 5 (Climax):** The ultimate test of consequence.
- **Stage 6 (Resolution):** Closure and character growth reflections.

## 3. UI Requirements
- **Horizontal Storyboard view:** A timeline show-casing the 6 stages, with the ability to add multiple "Scene Cards" per stage.
- **"Real-Time" Scene Editor:** An editor that discourages summary and encourages sensory detail and dialogue.
- **Element Blender:** A tool to check if a scene successfully mixes "Setting", "Character", and "Action".
- **Tension Meter:** A visual guide that tracks intensity across the stages.

## 4. Derived & Automated Fields
- **Cohesion Linker:** Identifies narrative threads that haven't been resolved across scenes.
- **Legacy Generator:** Suggested "Long-term impacts" based on Climax choices.

## 5. Exports & Integration
- Full Adventure Module (PDF).
