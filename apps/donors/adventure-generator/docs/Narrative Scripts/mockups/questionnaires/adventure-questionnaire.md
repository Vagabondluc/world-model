## 1. AI_QuestCreation_v1 (Narrative Co-pilot)

**1. What are the two primary roles in this interaction?**
The Storyteller (AI) and the Protagonist (User).

**2. How are "Simple" vs "Complex" tasks differentiated in the UI?**
- Simple: Effortless narrative resolution.
- Complex/Uncertain: Requires specific evaluation of abilities and a success/failure decision from the Storyteller.

**3. What is the "Consistency Check" requirement?**
The Storyteller must ensure interactions are plausible within the established context (e.g., no police in space unless a station is nearby).

**4. What unique narrative perspective is required?**
The UI must surface not just what the character perceives, but also background knowledge and subjective experience.

## 2. arcane_library_method (Adventure Seed Generator)

**1. What are the three columns of the Adventure Generator Table?**
Action, McGuffin, and Subject.

**2. How many initial premises are generated per session?**
Five (5) expanded adventure premises.

**3. What contextual development is required for the chosen seed?**
- Origin of Problem
- Unique Positioning of the heroes
- Stakes of failure

## 3. pointyhat travel adventure (Traveling Event System - TES)

**1. How is travel distance abstracted?**
Into three brackets: Close (1 event), Far (2 events), Very Far (3 events).

**2. What are the primary color codes for events?**
- Red (Combat)
- Blue (Roleplay)
- Yellow (Exploration)

**3. What are the combination "Combo" colors?**
- Purple (RP + Combat)
- Green (Exploration + RP)
- Orange (Combat + Exploration)
- White (All three)

**4. What is the central design philosophy of TES?**
Drama and excitement over realism (ignoring rations/distance for narrative impact).
