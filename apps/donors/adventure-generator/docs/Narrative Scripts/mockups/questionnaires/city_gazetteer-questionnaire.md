# UI Questionnaire: city_gazetteer

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


## 1. Primary Purpose
A comprehensive urban encyclopedia tool designed to create, maintain, and expand a detailed city gazetteer, emphasizing transportation networks, district identity, and "living world" dynamics.

## 2. Core Inputs
- **City Identification:** Name, Purpose, and overall vibe.
- **District Definitions:** Names, general character, and demographics.
- **Transportation Nodes:** Entry/Exit points and major thoroughfares.
- **Landmark Data:** 3-5 unique locations per district.
- **City Services:** 2-3 specific options for Inns, Shops, Banks, etc.
- **Adventure Sites:** Key points of interest and dungeons.

## 3. UI Requirements
- **District Manager:** Vertical navigation for switching between city neighborhoods.
- **Transit Network Modeller:** A tool to map connections between districts (Subterranean, Aerial, Magical).
- **Gazetteer Entry Editor:** A standardized template including Landmarks, Services, and Adventure Sites.
- **Scenic Encounter Generator:** A dedicated workspace for 20-30 non-combat "slice-of-life" events.
- **Background Event Timeline:** A separate tracking system for global news and developments that do not involve the PCs.

## 4. Derived & Automated Fields
- **Encounter Roller (d6 System):** An interactive button that checks for a scenic encounter and pulls from the local table.
- **Archive Manager:** Automatically saves versions of the gazetteer to track urban evolution.
- **Entry/Exit Log:** Summarizes all major transportation hubs in a central dashboard.
- **Service Quality/Cost Estimator:** Automatically categorizes services based on the district's demographic wealth.

## 5. Exports & Integration
- Full City Gazetteer (Markdown).
- Interactive District Handouts.
- VTT Map Labels & Legend.
- NPC News Feeds (for "Background Events").
