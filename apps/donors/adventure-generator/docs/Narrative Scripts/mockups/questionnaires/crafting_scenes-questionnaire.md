# UI Questionnaire: crafting_scenes

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


## 1. Primary Purpose
A directorial tool for TTRPG GMs to frame scenes with high dramatic relevance, skipping mundane travel/details and focusing on "Agendas" and "Bangs."

## 2. Core Inputs
- **Scene Agenda:** The specific dramatic question (e.g., "Can they convince the Queen?").
- **The Bang:** The initiating event that forces a meaningful choice.
- **Scene Setup:** Characters (Leads, Features, Extras), Objectives, and Environment.
- **Obstacles:** Specific hurdles that prevent a trivial resolution.
- **Transition Type:** (Sharp Cut or Abstract Time).

## 3. UI Requirements
- **Dramatic Agenda Console:** A high-visibility input for the scene's core purpose.
- **The Bang Generator:** A tool to draft or randomize a starting conflict.
- **Character Role Matrix:** Assigning NPCs to "Lead," "Feature," or "Extra" status.
- **Sharp Cut Manager:** A specialized selector for the "Start" and "End" points of the scene, skipping the filler.
- **Obstacle Architect:** A workspace to define what blocks the players from their objective.

## 4. Derived & Automated Fields
- **Mundane Detail Filter:** Uses AI to identify and suggest stripping out unimportant travel or minor logistics.
- **Second Lull Alert:** A timer or checklist to help the DM recognize when the agenda is resolved and it's time to "Cut Away."
- **Abstract Time Summarizer:** Takes a period of uneventful time and crafts a 1-sentence transition.

## 5. Exports & Integration
- Scene Frame Note (Markdown).
- Director's Cheat Sheet.
- VTT "Now Playing" Card.
