## 1. Golden Compass Suite (Adventure Framework)

**1. What are the core character ratings in this system?**
Action, Guts, Knowledge, Society, Wild, Crime (Ratings from 1-3 Diamonds).

**2. How is time pressure managed mechanically?**
Via a "Doomsday Clock" with hour penalties for specific actions or trigger events.

**3. What status effects must the UI track?**
Dizzy, Shocked, Broken, Bleeding, Poisoned, Brutal, and Embarrassed.

**4. What are the tier-based challenge levels?**
Basic, Critical, Extreme, and Impossible.

---

## 2. Three-Pass Adventure Method (Iterative Workspace)

**1. What are the three distinct workflow stages?**
- Pass 1: Narrative Structure & Framework (The Story)
- Pass 2: Mechanical Implementation (The Game)
- Pass 3: Presentation & Usability (The Table Handout)

**2. What are the "REQUIRED STOPPING POINTS"?**
Approval from the user is required at the end of each Pass before mechanics or formatting can be applied.

**3. How should the UI handle "Bullet Points"?**
The UI should enforce that bullet points are only used for summarization, leading with rich narrative prose first.

**4. What visual aids are requested by the method?**
Scene flowcharts, NPC portraits, location maps, and player handouts.

---

## 3. PromptCreator & Whimsy (Logic & Variance)

**1. What is the role of "Whimsy"?**
Introducing unexpected, creative, and slightly chaotic elements to the adventure to keep it fresh.

**2. How does the Prompt Creator integrate?**
It generates the specific instructions that drive the Ollama models for each pass of the adventure creation.
