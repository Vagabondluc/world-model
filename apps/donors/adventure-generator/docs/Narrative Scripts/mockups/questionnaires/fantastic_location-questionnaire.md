# UI Questionnaire: fantastic_location

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


## 1. Primary Purpose
A focused set-piece architect designed to create "Fantastical Locations" (scenes/encounters) with a heavy emphasis on evocative naming, interactive aspects, and sensory immersion.

## 2. Core Inputs
- **Location Concept:** The central idea (e.g., A bridge made of teeth).
- **Core Aspects (Limit 3):** Exactly three short, interactive phrases describing key features.
- **Scale and Age:** Dimensions and historical timeframe (Ancient, Primordial).
- **Sensory Palette:** Descriptions for at least three senses (Sight, Sound, Smell, etc.).
- **Character Backgrounds:** (Optional) Knowledge or history ties for specific PC types.

## 3. UI Requirements
- **Evocative Namer:** A tool to generate brief, descriptive 3-5 word titles.
- **Aspect Drafting Board:** Three dedicated input fields for the "Location Aspects" (5-10 words each).
- **Sensory Matrix:** A checkbox-driven panel to ensure at least 3 out of 5 senses are described vividly.
- **Interaction Planner:** Fields to draft 2-3 specific ways characters can engage with the environment.
- **Lore & Secret Vault:** Space for 1-2 hidden elements or pieces of history.

## 4. Derived & Automated Fields
- **Background Bridge Builder:** Automatically suggests ties to common character backgrounds (Religious, Arcane, Archeology).
- **Concise Word Counter:** A live counter to ensure the final description stays within the 300-400 word target.
- **Adjective Suggester:** Proposes high-impact evocative language based on the location's core concept.

## 5. Exports & Integration
- Fantastic Location Dossier (Markdown).
- VTT Scene Note / Handout.
- Narrative Prompt Card for the DM.
