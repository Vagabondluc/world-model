# UI Questionnaire: Megadungeon Campaign Planner

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


## 1. Primary Purpose
A high-level strategic workspace for designing complex, multi-level 'Megadungeons' that serve as the central hub for long-term TTRPG campaigns, balancing ecosystem logic, faction politics, and deep narrative arcs.

## 2. Core Inputs
- **Overarching Central Theme:** (The 'Why' and 'What' of the entire structure).
- **Megadungeon Origin/History:** (Chronological backstory).
- **Faction Framework:** (Names and dynamics of 3-5 major occupant groups).
- **Restoration Systems:** (Rules for restocking and ecological shifts).

## 3. UI Requirements
- **Vertical Level Navigator:** A side-bar representing the 10-20 dungeon levels, allowing for rapid switching between level data.
- **Faction Political Matrix:** A visual graph or table showing territorial control and inter-faction stances (Allied, Hostile, Neutral).
- **Ecological Economy Studio:** Fields for defining Resource Flow (Water, Food, Loot) and Habitat chains.
- **Narrative Arc Manager:** A tool to map multi-level 'Quest Arcs' and mysteries that link distant parts of the dungeon.
- **Difficulty heat-map:** A visual indicator showing the progression of challenge levels (using color-coding from 'Breather' to 'Deadly').

## 4. Derived & Automated Fields
- **Level Theme Generator:** Automatically suggests thematic variations based on the central concept and the level depth.
- **Faction Conflict Simulator:** Suggests 'Dynamic Shifts' (e.g., Faction A takes Level 4 from Faction B) over campaign time.
- **Restocking Suggestor:** Predicts how an area changes after players clear it based on the established ecosystem.

## 5. Exports & Integration
- Megadungeon Campaign Guide (Markdown Dossier).
- Level Connection Flowchart (SVG/Mermaid).
- Faction Loyalty & Territory Tracker (JSON).
