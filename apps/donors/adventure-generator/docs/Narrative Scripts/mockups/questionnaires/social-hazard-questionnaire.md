## 1. social_event.txt (Urban Intrigue Hub)

**1. What is the scale of the Guest List?**
10-20 key NPCs immediately relevant to the PCs.

**2. How is the location designed to facilitate social movement?**
It must have multiple "Areas" and "Zones of Activity" (balconies, private rooms) to allow groups to form and break apart.

**3. What are the three methods for sequencing the Main Event?**
- Linear sequence.
- Grab bag (random order).
- Multiple linear tracks.

**4. How is NPC knowledge managed?**
Via "Topics of Conversation" (Recent events, gossip, political debates) with 2-3 general viewpoints per NPC.

## 2. trap_prep.txt (Trap Architect)

**1. What are the 4 core components of a trap?**
- Clue (The tell)
- Trigger (The activation)
- Danger (The effect)
- Obscure (The hiding method)

**2. What is the "Number Matrix" requirement?**
A 3x4 matrix of random numbers (1-6) used to selectively cross-reference the Component Table.

**3. What is the "Stages of Activation" principle?**
Traps must be described in stages to give players time to react and avoid "instant lethality".

**4. How does the UI handle "Thematic Consistency"?**
A global "Theme" setting must apply to all clues, triggers, and descriptions (e.g., "Ancient Temple").
