## 1. Eldwyn_instant_generator.txt (Setting Nexus)

**1. What is the specific setting for this generator?**
The mining town of Eldwyn in the fallen kingdom of Barze (Forgotten Realms), characterized by cold, rugged terrain (Northern Quebec/Sweden style).

**2. What are the 5 categories of the Adventure Table?**
Hooks, Threats, McGuffins, Intrigues, and Places.

**3. What is the "Matrix" structure for generating outlines?**
A 5-column, 4-row matrix of numbers (1-6). Each row generates one complete adventure outline (Total of 4).

**4. How does the "Generation Tier" work?**
- First Gen: Up to 80 words per narrative.
- Subsequent Gens (after user confirmation): Up to 120 words per narrative.

**5. What level of detail is required for entities?**
Every key entity must be named, given an occupation (where relevant), and described with specific, granular details focused on "cause and consequence."
