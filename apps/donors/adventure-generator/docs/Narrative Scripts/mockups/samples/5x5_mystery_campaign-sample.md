# Campaign: The Shadow Regency

### 1. Master Campaign Overview
**Overarching Narrative:** The current King of Aethelgard was killed and replaced by a clockwork simulacrum six months ago. The "Shadow Regency" (a coalition of artificers and corrupt nobles) is ruling from the shadows, slowly dismantling the kingdom's defenses to allow an extra-planar invasion.

### 2. The Five Mysteries (The 5x5 Grid)
- **Mystery A: The Missing Heirloom.** A theft investigation in the capital that reveals a high-tech "repair kit" not sold to the public. (Hooks to B, C, D).
- **Mystery B: The Poisoned Well.** A regional plague that turns out to be "Clockwork Sickness" (mercury poisoning from simulacrum maintenance).
- **Mystery C: The Tower Heist.** An infiltration of the Artificer's Guild to retrieve a stolen ledger of "Regency" members.
- **Mystery D: The Secret Sect.** An investigation into a cult that worships "The Perfect Machine."
- **Mystery E: The Coronation.** The final confrontation at the King's Jubilee, where the simulacrum is to be upgraded for permanent rule.

### 3. Recurring Elements
- **NPC: Counselor Vane.** A nervous noble who appears in A, B, and C, eventually revealed as a Regency thrall.
- **Location: The Gearbox Tavern.** A neutral ground in the docks used as a meeting point for informants across all mysteries.
- **Theme:** The sound of a slow, ticking clock heard behind palace walls.

### 4. Inter-Mystery Connections
- **From Mystery A (Node 4):** A discarded invoice from the **Artificer's Guild** (Points to Mystery C).
- **From Mystery C (Node 2):** A ritual pamphlet for the **Church of Gears** (Points to Mystery D).
- **From Mystery D (Node 5):** A confession by a dying cultist about the **Coronation Plot** (Points to Mystery E).
