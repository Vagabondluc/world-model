### Target Answer: A Shadow

---

#### Easy Version:
"I follow you in the day, but hide when the sun goes down. I grow tall in the evening and disappear in the dark. What am I?"

#### Hard Version:
"I am the silent twin born of luminescence, yet I wither in the void's embrace. I have no depth, but I can cover a mountain. I mimic your dance but never touch the floor. What am I?"

---

### Logical Explanation:
- **Easy Version:** Focuses on the literal behavior of a shadow (following the person, disappearing at night, changing size based on the sun's angle).
- **Hard Version:** Uses more abstract concepts:
    - *Twin born of luminescence:* Shadows require light to exist.
    - *Wither in the void:* In total darkness (the void), a shadow cannot exist.
    - *No depth / Cover a mountain:* Physical properties of a shadow.
    - *Mimic your dance:* Reflects user movement without physical contact.
