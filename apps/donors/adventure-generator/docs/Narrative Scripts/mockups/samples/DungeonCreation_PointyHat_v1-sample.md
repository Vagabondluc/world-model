# Sample Output: Pointy Hat Dungeon - "The Vault of Rust"

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


### **Step I: Theme**
**RUST**

---

### **Step II: Mechanics**
- **Mechanical Fatigue:** Every time a non-magical metal item is used (attack, check, or hit), roll a d20. On a 1, the item gains a '-1' penalty to its effectiveness.
- **Oxidized Air:** Resting only provides half-benefits unless the party creates a sealed, rust-free environment.

---

### **Step III: Goal**
Retrieve the 'Ever-Polished Prism' from the heart of the Iron Cathedral to stop the creeping oxidation of the capital city.

---

### **Step IV: Encounters**
1. **Combat (The Iron Hounds):** Three clockwork beasts that prioritize attacking the players with the most metal equipment (e.g., Paladins, Fighters).
2. **Trap (The Mercury Rain):** A corridor where ceiling vents drip corrosive liquid; players must navigate a series of rusted, crumbling catwalks.
3. **Roleplay (The Scribe of Sand):** A ghost who refuses to share the vault code until the players help him polish his ancient brass ledger.

---

### **Step V: Ending (The Synthesis)**
The 'Prism' is guarded by a massive Rust Dragon. During the fight, the 'Mechanical Fatigue' increases (1-3 on d20). The dragon can be pacified if players 'offer' their most precious metal items to it, synthesizing the theme of loss and decay with the mechanic of item degradation.
