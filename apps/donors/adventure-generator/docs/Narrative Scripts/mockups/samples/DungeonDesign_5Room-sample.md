### **Room 1: Entrance & Guard (The Clockwork Gate)**
- **Mood:** Tense, clicking, smelling of oil.
- **Guardians:** Two Bronze Golems that only allow entry to those with the 'Sigil of Seconds.'
- **Hero Spotlight:** **Tech/Hacking:** The golems' control panel is exposed on the side, allowing a skilled PC to disable them without combat.
- **Transition:** Leads to the 'Flex: Trials' area.

---

### **Room 2: Flex Trial (The Maze of Shifting Sands)**
- **Challenge:** A non-combat trial where the floor panels rotate based on the sound of a ticking pendulum.
- **Hero Spotlight:** **Observation/Scholar:** Identifying the rhythmic pattern of the pendulum allows for safe passage.
- **Outcome:** Success grants a 'Feather of Finesse.' Failure triggers a PIT TRAP leading back to Room 1.

---

### **Room 3: Midpoint - The Twist (The Empty Throne)**
- **Summary:** The PCs reach the 'Chronomancer,' only to find it is a hollow mechanical shell being operated from the basement.
- **Setback:** The floor collapses (The 'Snake-infested floors' from the script), dropping them into the lower maintenance tunnels.
- **Theme:** Deception and Disruption.

---

### **Room 4: Climax/Battle (The Maintenance Core)**
- **Antagonist:** Master Tinker Vane.
- **Tactical Element:** Vane uses 'Prep Time' features to flood the room with grease, making the floor difficult terrain.
- **Hero Spotlight:** **Combat/Guts:** PCs must charge through the grease to reach the control lever while dodging Vane's laser-bots.

---

### **Room 5: Reward/Revelation (The Master Blueprint)**
- **Reward:** Vane's personal logbook, revealing that he was actually trying to *stop* a greater catastrophe.
- **Prophecy Revelation:** The log reveals that one of the PCs is mentioned in an ancient Chronomancer scroll.
- **Epilogue Hook:** "The ticking hasn't stopped... it's just gotten faster."
