**Adventure Title: The Clockwork Heart**
**Status: Drafted**

### Stage 1: Prep & Intro (Static)
- **Summary:** The party finds a dying automaton in the market. Its chest cavity contains a map to the "Central Engine".
- **Characters:** Unit-41 (Dying Bot), Market Bystanders.
- **Challenge:** Deciphering the bot's binary stutter (Intelligence check).
- **Transition:** Following the map leads to the ruined factory sewers.

### Stage 2: Overcoming Challenges (Flex)
- **Scene A (Combat):** Sewer Vermin Swarm. Scavenger rats infected with tech-bio-rust.
- **Scene B (Puzzle):** The Steam Pressure Hallway. Timing jumps through leaking pipes.

### Stage 3: Midpoint - The Twist (Static)
- **Summary:** The "Central Engine" isn't a factory part; it's a sentient artificial god being harvested for parts by the local Governor.
- **Challenge:** Realizing the moral choice: shutdown the engine (killing the bot-god) or free it (collapsing the city's power).

### Stage 4: Facing Roleplay Challenges (Flex)
- **Scene C (RP):** Negotiating with the Factory Foreman who is just trying to provide for his family.

### Stage 5: Climax & Confrontation
- **Summary:** Confronting the Governor in the heart of the engine room.
- **Challenge:** High-stakes combat with environmental hazards (the Engine's defense lasers).

### Stage 6: Resolution
- **Outcomes:** The Engine is freed; the city goes dark but the "god" survives.

### Stage 7: Epilogue
- **Hooks:** Unit-41 is reborn. The Governor flees to the mountains.
- **Final Note:** The party is now known as the "Breakers of the Light".
