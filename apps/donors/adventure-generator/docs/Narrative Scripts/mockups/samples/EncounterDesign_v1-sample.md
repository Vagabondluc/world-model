# Scene: Crossroads Ambush
**Goal:** Break through the bandit barricade and secure the supply wagon.

### 1. Description
Rain-slicked mud churned by wheels. The sharp tang of wet fur and cold iron. A massive fallen pine blocks the road, its branches stripped bare. Bandits lurk in the treeline, bows drawn.

### 2. Environment Hacks
- **Muddy Ground:** Difficult terrain (-10ft movement).
- **Heavy Rain:** Disadvantage on all Ranged Attacks beyond 30ft.

### 3. Challenge Steps
1. **Perception (DC 13):** Spot the archers hidden behind the fallen pine.
2. **Strength (DC 15):** A group effort to lever the log off the road (requires 2 successes).
3. **Intimidation (DC 14):** Attempt to scare the bandits into parley by displaying the Captain's severed banner.

### 4. Opponents & Tactics
- **4x Bandits:** Stay in the treeline; focus fire on anyone attempting to move the log.
- **1x Bandit Captain:** Stays mounted; charges the wagon if the log is moved.

### 5. Outcomes
- **Success (Log Moved):** Cargo reaches the village. **Hook:** The village elder reveals the bandits were actually local militia in disguise.
- **Failure (Wagon Stalled):** Cargo is looted. **Hook:** The party follows the wagon tracks to a hidden camp in the forest.

### 6. XP Rewards
- **Challenges:** 500 XP
- **Combat:** 1,500 XP
- **Total:** 2,000 XP
