### **Sudden Twist (Unexpected Detail):**
**Silas is a devout pacifist.** Despite his ruthless business reputation and association with criminals, he cannot stand the sight of blood and will actively sabotage violent plans.

---

### **Character Deep-Dive:**
Silas rose from the gutters of the Fog District not through strength, but through a terrifyingly sharp mind. He views the city as a series of accounts to be balanced. His white suit is both a status symbol and a practical tool—nobody expects a man in such expensive clothes to be hiding in an alleyway. He prizes information over gold, knowing that a single secret can buy a dozen kingdoms.
