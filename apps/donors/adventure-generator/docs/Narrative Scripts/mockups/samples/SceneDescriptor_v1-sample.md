# Scene: The Whispering Library

### Scene Description:
- **Ancient stone shelves stretching into the gloom.**
- **The smell of vanilla and rotting parchment.**
- **A single, flickering lantern illuminating a central desk.**
- **The rhythmic thumping of a heavy tome closing itself.**

*The library is a cathedral of silence, where the walls are formed of leather-bound memories stretching into the lightless heights. The air hangs heavy with the sweet, cloying scent of vanilla—the signature smell of slowly decaying scrolls—and the sharp bite of cold stone. In the center of the chamber, a lone brass lantern casts long, jittery shadows across a mahogany desk. Every few seconds, the silence is broken by a hollow 'thump' as one of the books on the high shelves slams shut of its own accord, as if refusing to be read.*

---

### Game Master Information:
This scene is a tension-builder. The players need to find the "Codex of Echoes" while avoiding the notice of the **Silent Librarian** (a spectral entity). The library is a maze, and the books closing is a warning that the Librarian is getting closer.

**Key Points for Running the Scene:**
- **Atmosphere:** Describe the shadows first. Make the lantern feel like the only safe place.
- **The Goal:** Finding the Codex requires a DC 15 Investigation check.
- **The Challenge:** Every 3 rounds of noise, the **Silent Librarian** appears.
- **Sensory Cue:** The smell of vanilla intensifies when the spectral entity is within 30 feet.
