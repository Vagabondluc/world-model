## [PASS 1] Narrative Framework
**Premise**: The heroes must infiltrate the 'Whispering Spire' of the Chronomancer before the Blood Moon rises.
**Objective**: Rescue the captured Heir of Valen.
**Rival**: Magnus Thorne, a disgraced wizard who seeks to rewind time and undo his past failures.

---

## [PASS 2] Mechanical Implementation
**Scene: The Spire Entrance**
- **Challenge**: The Timeless Barrier (CRITICAL Challenge).
- **DC**: 15 (Knowledge to bypass) or 18 (Guts to force through).
- **Action**: Magnus's goons (3 BASIC enemies) guard the gate.
- **Resource**: Costs 1 Wealth Point for local information, or 1 Hour on the Doomsday Clock.

## [PASS 3] Table-Ready Presentation

### The Spire Entrance
The Whispering Spire looms over the jagged peaks, its obsidian surface shimmering like oil on water. The air around the base vibrates with a low, dissonant hum that makes your teeth ache. A faint scent of ozone and burnt sage hangs in the air. High above, the Blood Moon begins its ascent, casting a crimson pall over the stonework.

**Key Features:**
- **The Timeless Barrier**: A shimmering wall of static energy.
- **Magnus's Sentry**: Three guards in red-and-gold livery.
- **The Obsidian Keyhole**: A small, star-shaped depression in the wall.

**Fortune Master Guidance:**
Players can bypass the barrier by solving the 'Riddle of the Sands' found in earlier scenes, or by a direct assault. If combat lasts more than 3 rounds, the 'Ticking Clock' adds a complication (Magnus notices the intrusion).

**Sentry Dialogue:**
"Nobody enters the Spire. The Master is... busy with history. Move along, or become a part of it."
