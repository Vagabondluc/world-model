# Travel: The Frozen Waste of Zann
**Distance:** Very Far (3 Events)

### 1. Journey Sequence (TES)
1. **[YELLOW - Exploration]**: Crossing the Glass Flats. DC 15 Acrobatics to avoid crystalline shards. 
2. **[PURPLE - Hybrid]**: Encounter with a starving Frost Giant. Negotiation or Combat?
3. **[RED - Combat]**: Ambush by the Rival's advance scout team (3x Guards).

### 2. Quest Seed (Arcane Library Roll)
- **Result:** "Deliver the Hunter to the pinnacle of the Mage's Spire."
- **Origin:** The Hunter is the only one who can track the Spire-Drake.
- **Unique Position:** The heroes possess the *Locket of Gravity*, necessary to scale the spire.
- **Stakes:** If they fail, the Drake will consume the city's archive.

---

### 3. Co-Pilot RP Log (Sample)
**STORYTELLER**: The wind howls against the obsidian stones of the spire. You can see the Hunter, Vax, shivering behind you. "We're almost there," he gasps. "Can you feel the air thinning?"

**PROTAGONIST**: I activate the Locket of Gravity and reach for a ledge. "Stay close, Vax. Don't look down."

**STORYTELLER (Decision Point)**: 
The ledge is slick with frost. This is a **Complex Task**. 
- **Check Required:** Athletics (Strength).
- **Evaluation:** If you succeed, you reach the next platform smoothly. If you fail, you slip, and Vax's weight nearly pulls you both off.
