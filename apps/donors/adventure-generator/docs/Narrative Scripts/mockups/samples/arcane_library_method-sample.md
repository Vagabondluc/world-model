### **Initial Premise:**
**RESCUE THE GOBLET** (Roll: 1-2) from **the clutches of a dark sorcerer** (Roll: 1-2).

---

### **Contextual Development:**
- **Origin of Problem:**
The Sorcerer Malakor has stolen the *Goblet of Life* from the Sun-Temple. He intends to use its restorative essence to fuel a ritual of lichdom, essentially draining the land's vitality to sustain his own undead soul.
- **Unique Positioning:**
The heroes are currently escorting the temple's fleeing acolytes and are the only ones close enough to Malakor's obsidian spire before the celestial alignment begins.
- **The Stakes:**
If the ritual is completed, the valley of Valen will become a barren wasteland, and Malakor will become an immortal, unstoppable tyrant leading a legion of shadows.

---

### **Personal Stakes / Direct Involvement:**
- **The Call to Action:** The dying High Priest hands the heroes a shard of the temple's sun-shield, which is the only thing that can bypass Malakor's necrotic barrier.
- **Personal Hook:** One of the characters (the Cleric) was a former student of Malakor before the sorcerer turned to darkness; this is their chance to finish what was started years ago.
