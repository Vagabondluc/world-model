### 3. Battlefield Description: The Boiling Tundra (Row 1)

**Setting Overview:** A high-altitude caldera where the volcanic heat clashes with arctic winds. The air is thick with the scent of sulfur and ozone.

**Elements & Impact:**
- **Basalt Spires (Elev):** Jet-black pillars rise 20ft above the caldera, offering perfect sniping positions but making climbers vulnerable to the wind.
- **Shifting Cinders (Terr):** The ground is a treacherous mix of hot ash and cold snow, reducing movement speed and potentially scorching light footwear.
- **Lava Cores (Hard Cover):** Dense, obsidian-encrusted cores block line of sight and provide total cover from the prevalent steam vents.
- **Steam Vents (Soft Cover):** Frequent bursts of hot mist obscure vision across the central valley, providing concealment for stealthy movement.
- **Geothermal Jets (Danger):** Without warning, jets of superheated steam erupt from the floor. Characters must time their movements or risk fire damage.

**Tactical Opportunities:**
Ranged attackers will want to control the **Basalt Spires**, while melee combatants can use the **Steam Vents** to close the distance without being seen. The **Lava Cores** create a natural choke point around the central **Geothermal Jets**, forcing players to choose between the safety of cover and the risk of environmental damage.

**Battlefield Dynamics:** 
The primary dynamic is **Risk vs. Visibility**. The best cover is located near the worst terrain, and the best high ground is surrounded by hazardous jets. It is a high-tension arena that punishes static positioning.
