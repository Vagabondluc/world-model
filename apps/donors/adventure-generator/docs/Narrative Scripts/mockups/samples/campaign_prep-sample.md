# Campaign: Chronicles of the Hollow Crown

### 1. Campaign Overview
**Concept:** A Dark/High Fantasy epic focusing on the decay of a once-great empire and the discovery that the "God-King" has been a hollow shell for a century.
**Fantasy Flavor:** A blend of **Dark Fantasy** (horror elements in the decaying cities) and **High Fantasy** (ancient, over-powered artifacts).
**Length:** 30 Sessions, Levels 5-15, intended for a 1-year weekly campaign.
**Tier:** **Tier 2 to Tier 3**. Starts with regional politics and ends with national reality-warping conflicts.

---

### 2. Setting and Theme
**Setting:** The Empire of Ostia, a land of massive marble cities now being reclaimed by a purple "Aether-Rot."
**Main Themes:**
- **Corruption of Power:** The deeper the players delve into the capital, the more they see how magic has rotted the soul of the ruling class.
- **Redemption:** Can the Ashen Princess be saved, or is she too far gone into the Rot?

---

### 3. Metaplot (Key Milestones)
1. **The Fall of Raven-Watch:** The first regional city is fully consumed by the Rot.
2. **Meeting the Exile:** Players discover that the true heir is alive but hideously scarred.
3. **The Silent Throne:** Discovery that the King is physically hollow, filled only with Rot-Mist.

---

### 4. Three-Act Arc
- **Act 1: The Gathering Storm.** Players deal with local Rot-cults. *Climax:* The Siege of Raven-Watch.
- **Act 2: The Path of Thorns.** Finding the three keys to the capital. *Climax:* Confronting the Rot-Marshal.
- **Act 3: The Crown of Echoes.** Infiltrating the capital. *Climax:* Final choice—cleanse the land or inherit the Rot.

---

### 5. GM Advice (The "Flexibility" Side)
**Branching Point:** If the players side with the Rot-Cult in Act 1, the campaign pivots into an "Evil" campaign where they are the harbingers of the new age.
**PC Integration:** Tie the Rogue PC's missing father to the "Steward of the Hollow Throne" to ensure personal stakes in the final act.
