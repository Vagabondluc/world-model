### **2. Surface Profile (Français)**
**Nom:** Theron l’Encré
**Occupation:** Scribe de ruines
**Apparence:** Un demi-elfe aux mains tachées d'encre arcanique. Il porte des lunettes de cuivre et une cape de parchemin.
**Personalité:** Calme et méticuleux, Theron préfère le silence des vieux livres au tumulte des tavernes.

---

### **3. Deep Backstory (Extract)**
- **Place of Birth:** The Floating Scriptorium of Valen.
- **Current Residence:** A cramped cellar in the Eldwyn Mining Outpost.
- **Psychology:** Theron is highly introverted. Happiness to him is deciphering a previously 'unbreakable' sigil. He fears history repeating its worst cycles because of lost knowledge.
- **History:** Raised among the immortal scribes, he was exiled for stealing a 'Forbidden Verb.' His earliest memory is the smell of crushed lavender and ozone in the scriptorium's main hall.
- **Physical Ailment:** His left hand is permanently stained black and occasionally 'twitches' as if writing in the air when he is nervous.

---

### **4. Character Growth Arc (Hero's Journey)**
- **Status Quo:** Theron works as a low-level clerk in Eldwyn.
- **Inciting Incident:** He discovers a mining map that uses the 'Exiled Verb' as a coordinate.
- **Call to Adventure:** Silas Vane asks him to decrypt the "Shadow Vein."
- **Mirror Moment:** Theron realizes the Verb isn't just a word, but a living entity that he has been housing in his 'Twitchy' hand.
- **Resolution:** Theron uses the living Verb to close the portal in the Sunless Sea, losing his ability to speak but finally reconciling with his exile.
