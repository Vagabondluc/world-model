# Sample Output: The Frozen Chasm Encounter

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


### **1. Combat Arena: The Weeping Gorge**
- **Location:** An icy canyon where walls are constantly shedding slabs of frost.
- **Physical Features:** Slippery Ledge (DC 13 Acrobatics), Icy Stream (Difficult Terrain).
- **Environmental Hazard:** Falling Ice Slabs (Erupt every 3 rounds).
    - **Trigger:** Roll a d6; on a 5-6, the ledge on the East side collapses.
    - **Effect:** 3d6 bludgeoning damage, DC 14 Dex save for half.
- **Detailed Tactics:** Frost Wraiths (Artillery) stay 30ft away, firing 'Soul Bolts.' They move *behind* the Falling Ice Slab triggers, luring players into the danger zone.

---

### **2. Encounter Balance (Silver Rank)**
- **Party:** 4 players at level 4.
- **Thresholds:** Easy (500), Medium (1000), Hard (1500).
- **Enemies:** 2x Frost Wraiths (450 XP each) + 2x Ice Mephits (100 XP each).
- **Multiplier:** x2 (Small Group).
- **Total Adjusted XP:** 2,200 XP.
- **Status:** **HARD** (Pushing the limits for a Silver rank session).

---

### **3. Generated Trap: The Clicking Floor**
- **Clue:** A rhythmic, faint clicking sound from beneath the tiles.
- **Trigger:** Heavy pressure (Placing a pack or stepping firmly on a primary tile).
- **Danger:** Volley of poisoned crossbow bolts (2d8 piercing + poisoned condition).
- **Obscure:** The trigger tile is textured exactly like the surrounding slate.
- **GM Note:** Give the players one "Stage" of reaction: "The floor clicks and you feel the tile sink 2 inches. What do you do?"

---

### **4. Urban Crawl Layer: The Scribe's District**
- **Layer:** 'The Exiled Verb' Mystery.
- **Investigation Action:** A DC 14 Investigation check reveals that Theron the Inky was seen meeting a hooded figure at the 'Ink & Talon' tavern.
- **Triggered Event:** If the players enter the Shadow Market, they are immediately approached by a collection agent of the Faction 'Copper-Vane.'
