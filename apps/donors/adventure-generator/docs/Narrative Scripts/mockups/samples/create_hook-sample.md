# Scenario Hook: The Whispers of the Silvercross Manor

**Hook Type:** Twisted Hook
**Scenario Overview:** An investigation into a "haunted" manor that is actually the feeding ground for a fledgling vampire.

### Foundation:
- **Location:** Silvercross Manor, a lonely estate overlooking the Salt-Bluff.
- **NPCs:** Lord Byron (the master), Elisa (the maid/patron), The "Grey Lady" (the supposed ghost).
- **Core Conflict:** Lord Byron has become a vampire and is slowly consuming his staff, using the legend of a family ghost to cover his tracks.

### The Hook:
A frantic young woman named Elisa approaches you in the tavern. Her hands tremble as she presents a blood-stained handkerchief. "The Grey Lady has returned," she sobs, her voice a ragged whisper. "She took the stable boy last night. You can hear her wailing behind the bricked-up doors of the cellar. Please, before she takes me too!"

### Depth & Intrigue:
- **The Twist:** The "wailing" isn't a ghost; it's a surviving victim trapped in a blood-storage room.
- **Hidden Agenda:** Elisa knows more than she's saying. She's seen Lord Byron with blood on his chin but is too terrified to admit it, settling on the "ghost" story to get help without being accused of treason against her lord.

### Actionable Next Steps:
1. **Engage with Elisa:** She can be convinced to give you the cellar key if shown a sign of divine protection.
2. **Investigation Point:** Inspecting the Salt-Bluff cliffs might reveal where Byron "disposes" of the fully drained corpses.
3. **The Confrontation:** Byron invites the PCs to dinner to "discuss the ghost," actually intending to size them up as potential thralls.
