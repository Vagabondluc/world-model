# Sample Output: Dungeon Detail Suite - "The Silken Vault"

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


### **1. Brainstormed Concept**
- **Location:** An obsidian vault floating in a void of web-like nebulae.
- **Denizens:** Spectral spiders and semi-transparent 'Ooze-Ghouls'.
- **Goal:** Retrieve the 'Silk-Key' to unlock the passage to the Star-Core.
- **Twist:** The 'webs' are actually solidified threads of memory.

---

### **2. 10-Room Flow (JP Cooper Method)**
1. **Entrance Complication:** A field of 'Gravity-Grit'; metal gear becomes 2x heavier (Guts DC 15 to move).
2. **Logical Room:** The 'Hall of Forgotten Portraits' (Setting the spidery/memory tone).
3. **Environmental Complication:** The floor is made of 'Sticky Glass' (BASIC Danger of being restrained).
4. **Helpful Information:** A portrait whispers the secret way to avoid the Glass Pit in Room 9.
5. **Alternate Ally:** A trapped Spectral Spider named 'Xyx' who offers a shortcut in exchange for a 'Fresh Memory' (PC chooses a past event to forget).
6. **Secret Exit:** A mirrored surface that only reveals a reflection of the exit when seen through the Silk-Key.
7. **Resource Drain:** 'The Memory Drain'—a corridor that siphons 1 Luck Point unless the party moves quickly (1 Hour on Doomsday Clock).
8. **The Secret:** A hidden cocoon containing a *Starlight Scimitar* (+1 to Action vs Oozes).
9. **Final Battle:** The 'Grand Weaver' (Giant Ethereal Spider) in a chamber of shifting gravity.
10. **Resolution:** The Weaver's death reveals the path to the nebulae beyond, and a hook for the 'Void Trek' adventure.

---
