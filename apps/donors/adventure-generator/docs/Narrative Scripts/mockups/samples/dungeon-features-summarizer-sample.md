### Global Settings: The Obsidian Citadel
**Base Style:** Volcanic Fortress

**1. Illumination**
- **Type:** Low light.
- **Source:** Floating embers and veins of glowing magma behind reinforced glass.
- **Vibe:** Pulsing, flickering shadows.

**2. Physical Dimensions**
- **Ceiling Height:** Average 20ft (Vaulted).
- **Corridor Width:** 15ft (Designed for marching soldiers).
- **Wall Material:** Polished obsidian blocks, cold to the touch.
- **Floor Material:** Smooth basalt slabs.

**3. Environment**
- **Temperature:** Oppressively hot (Sweltering).
- **Sound:** The rhythmic "thrum" of a geothermal generator.
- **Smell:** Sulfur and burnt ozone.

**4. Architectural Details**
- **Doors:** Heavy stone slabs that slide vertically into the ceiling. Operated by pressure plates or lever-pulley systems.
- **Secret Passages:** Rare. Usually hidden behind wall tapestries or False Magma veins.

**5. Unique Feature**
- **Magical Gravity:** Every 10 minutes, gravity shifts slightly, making wall-walking possible for those attuned to the Citadel's core.
