### **Room 5: The Shimmering Maw**
**Boxed Text:**
*The air here is unnaturally cold, carrying the faint scent of crushed lavender and old parchment. Across the center of the room, a jagged pit filled with obsidian shards glints in the purple light. A rhythmic, low-frequency hum vibrates through the soles of your boots.*

**Skill Checks:**
- **Perception (DC 15):** Notice the silver tripwire at ankle-height near the pit.
- **Knowledge (Arcana) (DC 18):** Recognize the hum as a proximity sensor for the statues.

**Room Elements:**
- **Obsidian Maw (Pit):** A 20ft deep drop filled with glass spikes (2d6 piercing).
- **Starlight Guardian:** A statue of an elf that tracks the players' movement with glowing eyes.
- **Irrelevant Detail:** A small, discarded wooden child's toy sits in the corner.
- **Irrelevant Detail:** The walls are damp, but the moisture is warm to the touch.

**Denizens:**
- **2x Shadow Stalkers:** They cling to the ceiling and drop down if the tripwire is hit. [Aggressive].

**Connections:**
- **East:** Corridor leading to Room 6 (The Glass Bridge).
- **South (Secret):** A hidden rotating wall leading to Room 8 (The Inner Sanctum). Requires the 'Star-Sigil' to open.
