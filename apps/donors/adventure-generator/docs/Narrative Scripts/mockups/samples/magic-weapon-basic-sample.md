# Frost-Heart Blade
*Weapon (Longsword), Rare*

- **Cost:** 4,500 GP
- **Damage:** 1d8 (1d10) Slashing + 1d6 Cold
- **Properties:** Versatile, Magic
- **Weight:** 3 lbs.
- **Ability Bonus:** +1 to Attack and Damage rolls.

### Features
1. **Chill of the Grave:** On a critical hit, the target must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or be *Restrained* until the end of its next turn as ice encases its limbs.
2. **Giant's Breath:** Once per long rest, you can use an action to exhale a 15ft cone of frost. Each creature in that area must make a DC 15 Dexterity saving throw, taking 4d6 cold damage on a failed save, or half as much on a successful one.

### Flavor Text
*“Forged from the frozen heart of a frost giant, this blade pulses with a pale blue light. The ground freezes wherever the tip touches, and the air growls with the memory of an ancient winter.”*
