### **1. Overarching Concept**
**Theme:** The Vault of Broken Time.
**Origin:** Built by an ancient race to house chronological anomalies; now partially submerged in a sunless sea of magical waste.

---

### **3. Faction Framework**
- **The Scrapper Guild:** Resource-hungry dwarves looking for 'Bronze-Logic' artifacts. [Neutral to Players].
- **The Deep-Eye Cult:** Fanatical devotees of a blind krakken in the flooded levels. [Hostile to Scrappers].
- **The Echo-Wraiths:** Manifestations of past dungeon explorers; guard the deep core. [Enigmatic].

---

### **4. Ecosystem & Restoration**
- **Economy:** Factions trade 'Chrono-Dust' for fresh water piped from Level 1.
- **Food Chain:** Gigantic cave-shrimps from Level 5 sustain the higher-level predators.
- **Restoration Logic:** If Level 3 is cleared of Scrappers, the Deep-Eye Cultists from Level 4 will expand their territory into the brass halls within 1d6 days.

---

### **5. Campaign Integration**
- **Arc Hook:** A PC's grandfather disappeared in Level 3 fifty years ago; they carry his rusted compass which points to the Chrono-Core.
- **Outside Adventure:** A local noble provides a 'Breath-of-Air' potion in exchange for an artifact from the flooded levels.
