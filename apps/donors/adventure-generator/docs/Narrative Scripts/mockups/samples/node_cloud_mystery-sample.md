# Scenario: The Velvet Conspiracy

### 1. Core Mystery Elements
- **The Crime:** The theft of the Regency Seal.
- **The Perpetrator:** Lady Isolde, the High Chancellor's daughter.
- **The Motive:** Blackmailing her father to stop the "Golem Draft."

### 2. Node Cloud Highlights (Total: 15 Nodes)
- **Start Node 1: The Royal Mint.** A break-in occurred but nothing was taken. Clue: A lavender-scented handkerchief.
- **Start Node 2: The Whispering Alley.** A fence is selling information about "Blue-Blood secrets."
- **Start Node 3: The Chancellor's Manor.** The site of the theft. Clue: A missing window-latch opened from the inside.

### 3. Reactive Node: The Silver Slipper Tavern
- **Base State:** A high-society club for the nobility.
- **Trigger:** If players investigate Node 2 too loudly.
- **Evolved State:** The tavern becomes "Closed for Private Event," now guarded by Isolde's personal mercenaries.

### 4. Timeline of Events
- **Day 1:** The Seal is stolen. Isolde hides it in the Mint Vault.
- **Day 2:** The Chancellor receives the first blackmail note. (Dynamic Event: Panic in the streets).
- **Day 3:** Isolde meets the Fence (Node 2) to frame a rival.
- **Day 5 (If no intervention):** The Golem Draft is canceled; Isolde flees the city with the Seal.

### 5. Factions
- **The Regency Watch:** Active in Node 1, 3, and 5. Seeking the thief.
- **The Unwound Rebels:** Active in Node 2 and 7. Wanting the Draft canceled.

- **Whispering Alley** connects to: **Silver Slipper**, **Prison**, **Bazaar**.
