# Mystery Node: The Informant's Message (MysteryNode_SilverHeist_Proactive_01.txt)

### 1. Node Overview (Trigger Conditions)
This node is triggered if:
- Players have visited Node A and B but missed the "Silver Ledger" (Clue 2 in Node B).
- Players spend more than 2 in-game hours debating their next move without taking action.
- The investigation has reached a "Second Lull" in Node C.

### 2. Integration Instructions
Introduce this via a **"Coded Crossbow Bolt."** As the players are resting or traveling between locations, a bolt thuds into a nearby wall with a parchment wrapped around it. The shooter is seen fleeing over the rooftops (Leading into the scene).

### 3. Dynamic Content Variations
- **Investigation: Early Phase.** The note is vague: "The Silver isn't where you think. Check the Docks."
- **Investigation: Mid Phase.** (Active) The note is specific: "Vane is meeting the buyer at the Silver Slipper Tavern tonight. Go to the back entrance."
- **Investigation: Late Phase.** The note is desperate: "The vault is being emptied. They leave at dawn. The password is 'Crescent'."

### 4. Adaptable Leads
1. **The Scent of Salt (Physical):** The parchment smells of the harbor (Points to Node C: Harbor Warehouse).
2. **The Wax Seal (Physical):** Bears the crest of Lady Isolde (Points to Node D: The Chancellor's Manor).
3. **The Scrawled Initials (Environmental):** Signed "V." (Points to Node E: The Final Confrontation with Vane).

### 5. NPC: "Ratty" the Snitch
If the PCs catch the shooter:
- **Motivation:** Self-preservation. He was hired by a rival to ensure the "Shadow Regency" fails.
- **Dialogue:** "Don't kill me! I'm just the messenger. The big guy... he's at the docks. That's all I know!"

### 6. Consequences
Using this node reduces the "Stealth Discovery" bonus for the final raid, as the Regency is now aware someone is snooping. However, it ensures the PCs have the necessary password to enter the vault.
