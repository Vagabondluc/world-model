# Sample Output: The Siege of Blackwood Tower (Prep)

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


### **1. Intelligence Briefing**
- **Target:** Blackwood Tower (Cursed Outpost).
- **Intel Gathered:**
    - **Weakness:** The southern basement door is rotting and vulnerable to bludgeoning.
    - **Threat:** Two "Void Sentinels" (Artillery) occupy the parapets.
- **XP Budget:** 1,200 XP Total.

---

### **2. Squad Manifest**
- **Squad Alpha (The Hammers):** 4 Dwarf Brutes. Objective: Bash the basement door. (Cost: 400 XP).
- **Squad Beta (The Shadows):** 2 Half-Elf Ambushers. Objective: Counter-snipe the Void Sentinels. (Cost: 300 XP).
- **Siege Engine (The ram):** 1 Mechanical Battery. Objective: Support the main gate breach if Alpha fails. (Cost: 500 XP).

---

### **3. Strategic Phases**
- **Phase 1: Infiltration.** Squad Beta climbs the eastern wall to disable sentries.
- **Phase 2: Breach.** Squad Alpha strikes the basement door simultaneously.
- **Phase 3: Conquest.** All squads converge on the central ritual chamber.

---

### **4. GM Notes (Contingency)**
- If Squad Alpha fails the breach, Squad Beta is the only squad in the tower. They must switch to "Sabotage" mode to buy time for the ram to arrive at the main gate.
