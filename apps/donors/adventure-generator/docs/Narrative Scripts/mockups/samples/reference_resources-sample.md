# Sample Output: The Scribe's Dilemma (Narrative Scene)

**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


### **1. Active Plot Engine: "Blackmail"**
- **Core Tension:** Agency vs. Coercion.
- **Meat:** Theron the Inky is being coerced into decrypting the Shadow-Vein by a faction that holds his "Forbidden Verb" theft as leverage.
- **Narrative Pressure:** Each day Theron complies, the "Living Verb" in his hand becomes more chaotic.
- **Failure State:** The Verb manifests publicly, causing religious panic in Eldwyn.

---

### **2. Applied Twists (Twist Deck Draw)**
- **Twist 1: Sympathetic Villain.** The blackmailer is using the knowledge to find a cure for a local plague.
- **Twist 2: Another Group Failed First.** The party finds the remains of a previous scribe who "complied too long" and was consumed by the Verb.

---

### **3. Tactical Encounter: The Scribe's Vault**
- **Opponent:** The Obsidian Warden (Base: Golem).
- **Injected Role: Controller.**
    - **Tactics:** Instead of just punching, the Warden uses 'Gravity Welts' (DC 15) to pull intruders toward the "Hazard Zones" in the vault.
- **Supporting Role: Minions (Ink-Swarms).**
    - **Tactics:** Swarms target the spellcasters to cause "Distracted" conditions.

---

### **4. Atmospheric Event (Wilderness/Grim)**
- **Event:** "The trail is marked by the smell of ozone and wet ash. You find a bird that has been petrified, not into stone, but into solid ink. It is still warm."
- **Tone:** Grim / Suspenseful.
- **Perspective:** Third Person Limited (Through Theron's eyes).
