# Hazard: The Frost-Lock Trap
**Theme:** Ice Citadel

### 1. Component Logic (Matrix 1-2-5-4)
- **Clue:** Faint mist escaping from floor seams.
- **Trigger:** Applying body heat to the silver handle.
- **Danger:** Flash-freeze (Restrained, 2d6 cold dmg).
- **Obscure:** Magical illusion of a simple latch.

### 2. Staged Resolution
- **Stage 1 (Warning):** The handle feels unnaturally warm for a moment, then a sharp hiss of cold air escapes.
- **Stage 2 (Action):** The air in the corridor begins to turn milky white and crystalline. 
- **Stage 3 (Impact):** The heroes must succeed on a DC 14 Dex save or be frozen in place.

---

### 3. DM Guidance
- **Social:** If players disengage from the King's toast, trigger "The Sabotaged Lights" immediately to build tension.
- **Hazard:** Creative use of *Prestigiditation* to cool the handle should bypass the trigger without a roll.
