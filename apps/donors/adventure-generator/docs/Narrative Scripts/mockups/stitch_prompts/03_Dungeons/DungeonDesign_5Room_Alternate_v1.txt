## 2. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS (LOGIC & STYLE)
# UI Explanation: DungeonDesign_5Room_Alternate_v1

## Component Architecture
- **Stage Navigation:** A vertical timeline acting as the primary navigation. Clicking a "Stage" (e.g., Intro, Flex Block) opens the corresponding editor.
- **Static Scene Editor:** A standard form with fields for Summary, Characters, Setting, Challenge, Outcomes, and Transitions.
- **Flex Block Container:**
    - A special container for Stages 2 and 4.
    - **Draggable Cards:** Each scene is a card that can be reordered via drag-and-drop.
    - **Interchangeable Logic:** Visual indicators showing that these scenes can occur in any order.
- **Global AI Controller:** A floating panel that can "Fill Next Stage" or "Generate Full Adventure Plan".

## Interaction Logic
- **Stage Interconnectivity:** The "Transitions" field in one scene can deep-link to the "Summary" of the next scene if it's a fixed path.
- **Non-Linear Mapping:** In Flex Blocks, transitions are treated as "Entry Points" (how they got here) and "Exit Points" (where to next), allowing for a mesh-like structure.
- **Adaptive UI:** If a scene is marked as "Combat Heavy", the Challenge field expands to show Encounter Difficulty (CR) calculation tools.

## Visual Design
- **Theme-Sync:** Background tints change based on the selected Stage (e.g., red for Climax, blue for Epilogue).
- **Responsive Layout:** Timeline collapses to a breadcrumb menu on smaller screens.

**Adventure Title: The Clockwork Heart**
**Status: Drafted**

### Stage 1: Prep & Intro (Static)
- **Summary:** The party finds a dying automaton in the market. Its chest cavity contains a map to the "Central Engine".
- **Characters:** Unit-41 (Dying Bot), Market Bystanders.
- **Challenge:** Deciphering the bot's binary stutter (Intelligence check).
- **Transition:** Following the map leads to the ruined factory sewers.

### Stage 2: Overcoming Challenges (Flex)
- **Scene A (Combat):** Sewer Vermin Swarm. Scavenger rats infected with tech-bio-rust.
- **Scene B (Puzzle):** The Steam Pressure Hallway. Timing jumps through leaking pipes.

### Stage 3: Midpoint - The Twist (Static)
- **Summary:** The "Central Engine" isn't a factory part; it's a sentient artificial god being harvested for parts by the local Governor.
- **Challenge:** Realizing the moral choice: shutdown the engine (killing the bot-god) or free it (collapsing the city's power).

### Stage 4: Facing Roleplay Challenges (Flex)
- **Scene C (RP):** Negotiating with the Factory Foreman who is just trying to provide for his family.

### Stage 5: Climax & Confrontation
- **Summary:** Confronting the Governor in the heart of the engine room.
- **Challenge:** High-stakes combat with environmental hazards (the Engine's defense lasers).

### Stage 6: Resolution
- **Outcomes:** The Engine is freed; the city goes dark but the "god" survives.

### Stage 7: Epilogue
- **Hooks:** Unit-41 is reborn. The Governor flees to the mountains.
- **Final Note:** The party is now known as the "Breakers of the Light".
