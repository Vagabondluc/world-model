## 2. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS (LOGIC & STYLE)
# UI Explanation: jp cooper 10 room dungeon

## Component Architecture
- **Guided Chat Interface:** The central focus. It mimics a messaging app. ChatGPT's prompts are left-aligned; user responses/actions are right-aligned.
- **Dungeon Ledger (Sidebar):** A vertical list that dynamically builds as the user answers. It shows the high-level "Theme" fields at the top and the "10 Rooms" below.
- **Smart Toolbar:** A contextual toolbar that appears above the input box.
    - **Buttons:** [Roll Once], [Roll 3], [Roll Genre...], [Undo Last].
- **Review Panel:** A full-screen overlay that appears when the user asks to "see the dungeon".

## Interaction Logic
- **Step-by-Step Flow:** The script forces a linear progression (one question at a time) to prevent cognitive overload.
- **"Roll" Interception:** When the user clicks "Roll", the UI sends a background command to the LLM to generate one or more creative options based on the previously entered data.
- **Contextual continuity:** Subsequent rolls are weighted to fit the "Location" and "Purpose" defined in the first section.
- **Hot-Swapping:** Clicking any room in the Sidebar allows the user to jump back to that point in the chat and change their answer (branching history).

## Visual Design
- **Theme-Responsive:** The chat bubbles and sidebar accent colors shift based on the "Genre" chosen (e.g., Parchment/Black for High Fantasy, Neon/Blue for Cyberpunk).
- **Pro-Tip Toasts:** Occasional tooltip popups that remind the user they can ask for "more flesh out" or "genre rolls".

# The Void-Keepers' Bastille
**Total Rooms:** 10

### Dungeon Theme
1. **Location:** A floating island chained above a churning vortex.
2. **Purpose:** To contain the "Void-Fire", a sentient entropy energy.
3. **Creator:** The Solari Inquisition (Celestial Order).
4. **Treasure:** Solar Quartz (used to fuel magic armor).
5. **Motivation:** The chains are breaking; if the island falls, the vortex consumes the world.

### 10-Room Key
1. **Entrance/Guardian:** A "Light-Bound Ward". A spell that requires a drop of innocent blood (or a high Arcana check) to pass.
2. **Logical Location:** The Hall of Cages. High ceilings with empty bronze cages used to hold corrupted souls.
3. **Environmental Complication:** Gravity Fluctuations. Every round, players may start floating or find their weight tripled.
4. **Helpful Information:** A dying Solari guard whispers: "The Void-Fire feeds on fear. Do not blink."
5. **Alternate Threat/Ally:** A Bound Demon who wants the Void-Fire for himself but will offer a safe path if promised freedom.
6. **Secret Exit:** A drainage pipe for solar-ichor; leads directly to the Chained Cliffs.
7. **Resource Drain:** The "Soul-Fog" - a mist that drains 1 Hit Die for every hour spent inside.
8. **Secret Room:** The Armory of Sol. Contains "Sun-Forged Plate" which grants immunity to the Soul-Fog.
9. **Final Conflict:** The Inquisitor of Embers. A corrupted celestial who has merged with the Void-Fire.
10. **Reward / Plot Twist:** The Void-Fire isn't actually a threat; it was a power source stolen from another world. The Inquisitors were the villains all along.
