## 2. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS (LOGIC & STYLE)
# UI Explanation: EncounterDesign_Older_v1

## Component Architecture
- **Vivid Header:** Allows for naming the scene and defining the "Tier" or player level to scale rewards.
- **Sensory-Rich Textbox:** A specialized input for "Setting the Scene" that encourages 3+ sensory details via live feedback tags (e.g., [Sight: Found], [Sound: Missing]).
- **Mechanism Matrix:**
    - **Environment Block:** Focused on mechanics and gameplay modifiers (e.g., difficult terrain, magical dimness).
    - **Challenge Logic:** A step-by-step editor for Skill Challenges or Puzzles, including DC settings and success/failure counters.
- **Tactical Dashboard:** Integration for opponent statblocks and AI-generated "Tactics Notes" that explain *how* NPCs use their environment.
- **Narrative Branching Panel:** Explicit fields for Success/Failure outcomes and the resulting "Narrative Hooks" for the next adventure phase.

## Interaction Logic
- **Outcome Mapping:** When a "Success" outcome is typed, the "Transition" field auto-suggests a logical next step (e.g., "Success in the chasm leads to the temple gates").
- **Dynamic XP Tally:** Changes as monsters or challenge complexity is adjusted.
- **Interactive Checklists:** Ensure the user has provided specific DCs and combat mechanics for every challenge listed.

## Visual Design
- **High Contrast Sections:** Uses clear bordered containers to separate "The Vibe" (Scene) from "The Math" (Mechanics).
- **Icon-Driven UI:** Icons for sensory categories (Ear, Eye, Nose) and status badges for "Combat" vs "Social".
- **Drafting Watermarks:** Sections like "Opponent Info" have watermark placeholders to guide the user on what to include (e.g., "Describe their motives...").

## 3. CONTEXT DATA (SAMPLE OUTPUT)
# Sample Output: EncounterDesign_Older_v1

# Scene: The Whispering Chasm

### 1. Setting the Scene
The air in the chasm is thick and humid, smelling of wet soot and ancient decay. Blue phosphorescent moss clings to the jagged obsidian walls, pulsing in time with a low-frequency hum that vibrates in your teeth. Far below, the "Whispering Winds" carry the distorted voices of those lost in the abyss.

**Primary Objective:** Retrieve the *Idol of Zann* from the central basalt pillar before the gravity vortex collapses.

### 2. Environmental Effect: Void-Fluctuation
- **Mechanics:** Every 2 rounds, gravity shifts. PCs must succeed on a DC 14 Strength saving throw or be pulled 15ft toward the nearest chasm edge. 
- **Physical Impact:** Hair stands on end; loose stones float upward before slamming back down.

### 3. Challenge: The Crossing (Skill Challenge)
- **Goal:** Reach the pillar. Needs 4 successes before 3 failures.
- **Skills:**
    - *Athletics (DC 15):* Navigating the floating rubble.
    - *Arcana (DC 18):* Predicting the next gravity shift.
    - *Insight (DC 12):* Interpreting the "Whispers" to find safe footing.
- **Failure Outcome:** A fall into the lower "Harpie Webs" (Restrained state).

### 4. Opponents: Harpy Matriarchs (3)
- **Tactics:** The harpies stay 40ft away, using their 'Luring Song'. They wait for the gravity to shift, then swoop in to push PCs who are off-balance.
- **Motive:** They believe the Idol's hum is the voice of their dead Mother Goddess.

### 5. Consequences
- **Success:** The party gains the *Idol of Zann*. Narrative Hook: The Idol starts weeping black ichor, pointing toward the *Obsidian Citadel*.
- **Failure:** The Idol falls into the vortex. The harpies capture the party. Transition: A prison break scenario in the harpy nests.

**Total XP:** 2,400 (Challenges + Opponents)
