## 2. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS (LOGIC & STYLE)
# UI Explanation: EncounterDesign_v1

## Component Architecture
- **Compact Header:** Combines Title and Primary Goal into a single line to maximize vertical space.
- **AI Fast-Fill Button:** A prominent action that drafts a complete encounter based on the Title alone.
- **Tiled Data Blocks:** Uses a two-column grid for Environment and Challenge steps, allowing the DM to read both simultaneously.
- **Tactics Bullet-List:** Specifically designed for "IF/THEN" logic, making NPCs easier to run in the heat of combat.
- **XP Summary Footer:** A sticky footer that shows the breakdown of Challenge XP vs Combat XP.

## Interaction Logic
- **Quick-Entry Mode:** Tabbing through fields focuses on the most essential "Practical Guidelines" first.
- **Inline Math:** When a monster is added to the "Opponents" list, its XP is automatically added to the "Combat" tally.
- **One-Click Hook:** The "Hook" areas are pre-populated with "Success/Failure" presets that can be swapped with a single click.

## Visual Design
- **Tabletop Focused:** Optimized for high-contrast viewing on small screens (tablets or phones) frequently used at the table.
- **Action-Oriented Symbols:** Bullet points use functional icons (e.g., crosshairs for tactics, a d20 for skill checks).
- **Cheat-Sheet Formatting:** Avoids long paragraphs in favor of bolded keywords and short sentences.

## 3. CONTEXT DATA (SAMPLE OUTPUT)
# Sample Output: EncounterDesign_v1

# Scene: Crossroads Ambush
**Goal:** Break through the bandit barricade and secure the supply wagon.

### 1. Description
Rain-slicked mud churned by wheels. The sharp tang of wet fur and cold iron. A massive fallen pine blocks the road, its branches stripped bare. Bandits lurk in the treeline, bows drawn.

### 2. Environment Hacks
- **Muddy Ground:** Difficult terrain (-10ft movement).
- **Heavy Rain:** Disadvantage on all Ranged Attacks beyond 30ft.

### 3. Challenge Steps
1. **Perception (DC 13):** Spot the archers hidden behind the fallen pine.
2. **Strength (DC 15):** A group effort to lever the log off the road (requires 2 successes).
3. **Intimidation (DC 14):** Attempt to scare the bandits into parley by displaying the Captain's severed banner.

### 4. Opponents & Tactics
- **4x Bandits:** Stay in the treeline; focus fire on anyone attempting to move the log.
- **1x Bandit Captain:** Stays mounted; charges the wagon if the log is moved.

### 5. Outcomes
- **Success (Log Moved):** Cargo reaches the village. **Hook:** The village elder reveals the bandits were actually local militia in disguise.
- **Failure (Wagon Stalled):** Cargo is looted. **Hook:** The party follows the wagon tracks to a hidden camp in the forest.

### 6. XP Rewards
- **Challenges:** 500 XP
- **Combat:** 1,500 XP
- **Total:** 2,000 XP
