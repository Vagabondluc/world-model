## 2. TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS (LOGIC & STYLE)
# UI Explanation: magic-item

## Component Architecture
- **Categorized Data Management:** Separate sections for General Identity, Magical Properties, and Lore/Advanced features.
- **Dynamic Property Builder:** An interactive list where DMs can add specific spells or mechanic-based abilities. It automatically suggests spell-slot costs based on item rarity.
- **Specialized Workspaces:** Context-aware modals for Sentient Items (stats/personality) and Artifacts (destiny/destruction).
- **Auto-Value Calculator:** A background engine that calculates recommended market values for items based on 5e rarity tables and item complexity.

## Interaction Logic
- **Constraint Enforcement:** If an item is set to "Uncommon," the UI will flag or reject Level 5+ spell effects to maintain balance consistency.
- **Fabled Scaling Engine:** For "Fabled" items, the UI provides a level-by-level progression table, allowing the DM to define when and how the item grows.
- **History Linker:** A "Generate Lore" button that analyzes the item's properties (e.g., Cold, Kraken-themes) and proposes historical origins that fit the theme.

## Visual Design
- **High-Fantasy Aesthetic:** Uses custom borders and scroll-like texture patterns in the preview area to evoke a "Magic Item Compendium" feel.
- **Interactive Charge Trackers:** Visual icons for charges that "light up" in the preview to show usage.
- **Status Badges:** Color-coded rarity badges (Blue for Rare, Purple for Very Rare, Gold for Artifact).

# Sample Output: magic-item

# Magic Item Concept: Helm of the Abyssal Tide

### 1. Item Name and Type
- **Name:** Helm of the Abyssal Tide
- **Type:** Wondrous Item

### 2. Purpose and Function
Created by the Marid lords to allow their favorite mortal champions to visit the Pearl Courts. It functions as a navigational aid and life-support system for those unused to oceanic pressures.

### 3. Physical Description
A bronze helmet encrusted with living coral and barnacles. The visor is made of a single piece of translucent green quartz. When worn, the user hears a faint sound of crashing waves, and the visor pulses with soft phosphorescence in deep water.

### 4. Magical Properties
- **Tidal Sight:** You gain darkvision out to a range of 120 feet. If you already have darkvision, its range increases by 30 feet.
- **Oceanic Favor:** While wearing the helm, you can breathe underwater and have a swimming speed equal to your walking speed.

### 5. Activation and Usage
- **Command Word:** *“Zann”* (Aquan for "Home")
- **Charges:** 5 charges.
- **Maelstrom (3 Charges):** Use an action to cast *Maelstrom* (Spell DC 16). The helm recharges 1d4+1 segments at dawn, provided it has been submerged in salt water for at least one hour during the previous day.
- **Attunement:** Requires attunement.

### 6. Rarity and Value
- **Rarity:** Very Rare
- **Estimated Value:** 25,000 GP

### 7. History and Lore
Worn by Admiral Kaerwen during the Siege of the Sunken Spire. It was lost for three centuries in the trench of the Deep Blue until found by a curious giant octopus.

### 8. Special Considerations
- **Cursed:** If the helm is not submerged in salt water for 24 hours, the wearer suffers one level of exhaustion as they dehydrate unnaturally fast.
