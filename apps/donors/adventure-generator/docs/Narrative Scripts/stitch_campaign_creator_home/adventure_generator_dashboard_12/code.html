Quick Reference Summary

This Dwarven Fort is characterized by its smooth obsidian walls and a chill, damp atmosphere smelling of wet slate.

Navigating the zig-zagging corridors is made difficult by the dim bio-luminescence, providing disadvantage on Perception checks relying on sight.

DM Note: Trigger the Runic Nodes if the party spends more than 10 minutes in any single junction.
