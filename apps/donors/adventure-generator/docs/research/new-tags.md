# 🔥 FIRE TAG

### **Passives**

* **Ignite Aura:** Weapon attacks deal +`(CR/2)` fire damage.
* **Heat Body:** Resistance to fire; vulnerability to cold.
* **Burning Terrain:** Moving adjacent causes 1 fire damage (minion deterrence).

### **Actives**

* **Flame Jet:** 15-ft line, Dex save (DC = 10 + CR), `2d6 + CR` fire.
* **Burst of Sparks:** 10-ft burst; creatures get **Distracted** (next attack at disadvantage).

### **CR-Upgrades**

* **CR 5+: Fire Shield**
* **CR 10+: Explosive Blood (hit → 1d6 fire)**
* **CR 15+: Lava Step (leaves molten squares dealing 3d6)**

### **Hybrid Unlocks**

| Combo          | Power                                                          | Type          |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------- |
| Fire + Air     | **Combustion Cyclone**: swirling burning tornado               | AoE / Terrain |
| Fire + Earth   | **Magma Pulse**: molten eruption; difficult terrain            | Heavy         |
| Fire + Shadow  | **Ash Veil**: smoke invisibility                               | Stealth       |
| Fire + Psychic | **Pain Ignition**: burn damage increases on frightened targets | Damage        |
| Fire + Water   | **Steam Burst** (yes, steam!)                                  | Blindness     |
| Fire + Arcane  | **Counterheat**: fire retaliation vs spells                    | Anti-magic    |
