Boxed Text

The heavy bronze doors groan as they yield, revealing a vast octagonal chamber bathed in a spectral violet luminescence. In the center, a colossal clockwork orrery suspended from the vaulted ceiling whirs with agonizing precision. Its silver planets trace paths through the air, casting elongated, jittering shadows that dance across the cracked marble floor like ghostly spectators. The air is thick with the scent of ozone and the relentless, rhythmic ticking of ancient gears, each click echoing against the walls like the heartbeat of a dying god.
