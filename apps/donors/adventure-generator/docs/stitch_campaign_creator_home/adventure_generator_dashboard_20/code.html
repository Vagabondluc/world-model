BATTLEFIELD GENERATOR // ACTIVE RESULT ROW 1: THE BOILING TUNDRA

Setting Overview

A desolate expanse of permafrost where volcanic vents tear through the ice. The air is a lethal mixture of freezing gale and scalding steam. Visibility is intermittent, and the very ground beneath your feet is prone to sudden, violent shifts in temperature and stability.

layers Elements & Impact

  • Elevation: Jagged Ice Plateaus

    Requires Athletics (DC 14) to scale. Grants +2 bonus to ranged attack rolls against targets in the lower steaming flats.

  • Terrain: Permafrost & Steam Vents

    Difficult terrain. Creatures ending their turn within 5ft of a vent must make a DC 13 DEX save or take 1d8 fire damage.

  • Cover: Obsidian Pillars

    Hard cover. Provides +5 to AC and DEX saves. These pillars can be shattered by heavy impact (AC 15, 30 HP).

strategy Tactical Opportunities

Vent Exploitation: Clever combatants can use forced movement (shove, thunderwave) to push enemies into active steam vents for environmental damage.

Vertical Dominance: The central ice plateaus offer clear lines of sight over the obsidian pillars, negating most ground-level cover for aerial or ranged units.

Thermal Pockets: Staying near the vents prevents the 'Frozen' condition but risks burn damage; a balance of risk vs reward for long encounters.

Recommended Enemies: Remorhaz, Ice Mephits, Frost Giants.
