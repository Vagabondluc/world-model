Environmental Effects

Shifting Gravity

The ley lines beneath the chasm flicker irregularly. At the start of each round, roll a d6. On a 1, gravity reverses for 1 round. Creatures must succeed on a DC 14 Dexterity save or fall "upwards" 30 feet.

3x Harpy Matriarchs

  • AC: 15 (Natural Armor)

Tactical Behavior

The Matriarchs stay 30ft above the chasm, using their Luring Song to pull characters off the ledge. If gravity reverses, they use their 'Dive' reaction to grab restrained targets and drag them into the void. They flee if two of their number fall.
