The Crushing Slab

"As the center tile yields with a hollow 'thunk', the groaning of ancient chains echoes through the hall. Above, a shadow grows larger, the dust of centuries shaking loose from a ceiling that is no longer stationary."

Resolve Challenge

Perception DC 16

RUNNING THE TRAP

priority_high
1. Warning

Players notice the Clue. Passive Perception check against DC 16. Grant a 'gut feeling' to those who succeed.

play_arrow
2. Action

Trigger is met. Roll Initiative. The trap acts on Count 20. Players have one round to react or disable.

close
3. Impact

The slab drops. Failed saves result in full damage and Restrained condition until freed by a Strength check.
