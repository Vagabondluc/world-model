Corridor Description

STORYTELLER

Base Layout

The corridor stretches 60 feet, carved directly from the living granite of the mountain. Massive stone pillars, etched with geometric dwarven runes, flank the passage every 10 feet. The ceiling vaults into a series of pointed arches, rising to a height of 20 feet.

Sensory Details

  • The air is thick with the scent of wet slate and ancient charcoal.
  • A rhythmic, distant "thrum" vibrates through the stone floor.
  • Small, bioluminescent lichen casts a faint indigo glow in the corners of the arches.
  • The temperature drops noticeably as you progress further into the draft.

Lore / History

These halls were once the main artery of the Khazad-Gathol forge complex. The scratches near the base of the pillars are not from wear, but marks left by the Retreat of the Fifth Era, where thousand of refugees fled the advancing shadow.

Environmental Hazard

Loose Vaulting: Decades of seismic activity have weakened the archway at the 30-foot mark. Heavy impact or loud noises may trigger a rockfall (DC 14 Dex Save).
