**Version:** 0.1.0
**Status:** Draft
**Owner:** TBD
**Last Updated:** 2026-02-04


### **Epic:**

*As a Game Master, I want to use a multi-stage Narrative Encounter Designer that treats encounters as mini-stories, so I can create rich, dramatic, and mechanically sound scenes that integrate seamlessly into my adventure.*

---

## **User Story**

**As a GM**,
I want a guided, step-by-step workflow that builds encounters as dramatic narrative arcs,
so that each encounter contains:

* a setup
* rising tension
* a twist
* the core challenge
* the climax
* the aftermath

…and so that I can generate both **mechanical structure** (procedural) and **narrative texture** (AI-enhanced), and merge them into a final scene usable in my adventure.

---

## **Value**

*   Gives GMs tools to craft **memorable, story-driven encounters** rather than static fights.
*   Ensures every encounter has **emotional pacing**, **mechanical clarity**, and **world relevance**.
*   Produces content that can be reviewed, edited, saved, linked to the map, and reused.

---

## **Success Criteria**

A GM can:

1.  Navigate between the six encounter stages, completing them in any order or skipping stages for simpler scenes.
2.  Provide context via simple controls.
3.  Generate procedural outputs instantly and offline.
4.  (Optionally) ask AI to narrativize or expand content.
5.  Merge procedural + AI into a final edited scene.
6.  Save the multi-part encounter to their adventure compendium.
7.  Attach the encounter to an **Adventure Stage** or **Hex Map location**.
