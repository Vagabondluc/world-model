# III. Powers & Costs

## The Power System
Each player maintains a running total of **Power Points**.

*   **Income**: Roll **2d6** at the start of each round.
*   **Low Power Bonus**: If you end a turn with **5 or fewer** points, you gain a cumulative **+1 bonus** to your income for the *next* round (max +3). This rewards spending rather than hoarding.

## Power Descriptions

*   **Shape Land**: Create or modify terrain (mountains, lakes, forests, etc.) in a ~1 inch area.
*   **Shape Climate**: Change weather (snow, rain, desert heat).
*   **Create Race**: Define a new fantasy race (Elves, Orcs, etc.) and their starting location.
*   **Create Subrace**: Splinter group from an existing race (High Elves -> Dark Elves). Must start properly adjacent.
*   **Command Race**: Influence a race to act (start war, settle territory). Requires a presence (Avatar/Order).
*   **Command City**: Direct a city to build a wonder, wall, or raise an army. (Max 1 army per city per turn).
*   **Advance City/Civ**: Grant expert knowledge (Steel, Magic, Sailing). The civ becomes the world leader in this field.
*   **Purify/Corrupt**: Shift alignment (Good/Evil) by 1 step.
*   **Event**: Random fortune or misfortune (Gold mine found, plague, storm).
*   **Create Order**: Create a guild, sect, or organization within a race.
*   **Command Order**: Make an order act. Cost increases if you didn't create it (+5 or +10).
*   **Create Avatar**: A powerful individual (Dragon, Hero, High Priest). Can be resurrected for 5 points if slain.
*   **Command Avatar**: Avatars can perform "Immortal" or "Mortal" actions (like leading an army) cheaply.
*   **Catastrophe**: Wrath of the gods. Destroy stuff (volcanoes, earthquakes).
