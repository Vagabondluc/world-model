# V. Making Use of Your World

Once the Ages are complete, you have a rich, history-filled world.

## Recording History
One player (the "Venerable Bede") should record actions each turn.

*   **Format**: Turn Number, Player Name, Short Description of Action.
*   **History Chart**: A simple log is sufficient (see below).

## Post-Game Options
1.  **Map Making**: Have an artist friend turn the rough game map into a polished fantasy map.
2.  **Writing**: Use the history log as an outline for a novel or setting guide.
3.  **RPG Campaign**:
    *   Players can play their **Avatars** as high-level characters.
    *   **Round-Robin**: Since everyone knows the history, players can take turns GMing different eras or regions.

## Example History Chart
```
Age: ______ (First/Second/Third)
World Name: ________________

Turn | Player | Action
--- | --- | ---
1 | Bob | Shaped mountains in the North (9 pts)
1 | Alexi | Created forest on South coast (3 pts)
...
```
