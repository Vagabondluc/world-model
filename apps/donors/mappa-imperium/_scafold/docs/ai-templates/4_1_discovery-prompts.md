# Age of Discovery Event Prompts

## Core Requirements for All Responses:
- Provide specific, granular details about the discovery
- Create original names for all people, places, and artifacts
- Connect to established faction themes
- Include at least one unexpected consequence
- Suggest future story implications

---

## 3. Magical Event
"Your faction has discovered a powerful magical relic. Detail:
- Its physical appearance and unique properties
- The specific circumstances of discovery
- Three immediate effects on the discoverers
- Two potential uses for your faction

Example focus: How does finding the Whispering Codex of Merakshi affect your mining operations?"

## Usage Notes:
1. Each response should:
   - Connect to the faction's established identity
   - Provide specific named individuals involved
   - Include detailed location descriptions
   - Suggest multiple development possibilities
   - Create clear cause-and-effect relationships

2. When naming elements:
   - Use original, evocative names
   - Connect names to local geography/culture
   - Create consistent naming patterns
   - Avoid reference to existing fiction

3. For each discovery:
   - Immediate impact
   - Short-term consequences
   - Long-term possibilities
   - Integration with existing elements
