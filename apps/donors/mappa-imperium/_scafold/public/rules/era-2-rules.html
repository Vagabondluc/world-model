Era II: Age of Myth ⚡

The story of your world begins here with the development of a pantheon. Each player will create a number of deities that inhabit their home region. These gods may be worshipped, feared, or ignored, and will help to spark the imagination for the development and story of your world.

2.1 Number of Deities

Roll 1 die to determine the number of deities for your home region.

2.2 Domain

Roll 1 die to determine a domain for each deity by consulting the .

2.3 Symbol

Roll 1 die to determine a symbol for each deity using the .

2.4 Name

Create a name for each deity, then place all 3 (name, domain, symbol) together in either the margins of the map or on a separate sheet of paper.

If you need help coming up with a name, roll 2 or 3 times on the and combine the results.

2.5 Sacred Sites

Each player will take turns creating and naming sacred locations and items in each Home Region. Roll on the a number of times equal to the number of deities in your Home Region, then draw in the results. Label each site for the deity they represent.
