# Settlement Development Prompt

"Using your faction's characteristics and the rolled settlement purpose, describe how this location manifests these elements while serving its designated function. Consider how it reflects your people's nature and adapts to its environment."

## Required Input
- Settlement Purpose (from Settlement Table)
- Faction Details (Race, Symbol, Colors, Industry)
- Geographic Location/Features
- Any relevant heroes or events

## Narrative Structure

### Opening Overview (2-3 sentences)
Establish the settlement's:
- Name
- Primary visual impact
- Most distinctive feature
- Immediate sensory impression

### Core Function (1-2 paragraphs)
Describe how the settlement:
- Serves its primary purpose
- Adapts to local geography
- Reflects faction culture
- Manages resources

### Daily Life (1 paragraph)
Detail:
- Notable residents
- Regular activities
- Social organization
- Cultural practices

### Connections (1 paragraph)
Explain its:
- Relationship with other settlements
- Trade patterns
- Strategic importance
- Future prospects

## Example Response:

Given Input:
```
Settlement Purpose: Military
Faction: Mountain Dwarves
Symbol: Spider
Location: Coastal Cliff Face
Industry: Fine Carpentry
```

---

Webweave Citadel rises from the cliff face like a giant spider's web cast in stone, its concentric levels connected by both traditional stairs and intricate pulley systems. The fortress's most striking feature is its network of retractable bridges, crafted from web-pattern latticed woodwork that can be raised or lowered as needed for defense.

The citadel serves as both a military academy and a coastal defense hub. Master Architect Thela Silkweaver revolutionized military architecture by incorporating principles from spider web construction into the fortress's design. Each level contains specialized training areas where soldiers learn to fight in vertical environments, using silk-reinforced wooden equipment crafted in the settlement's unique workshops. The outer walls feature carefully positioned holes that create defensive whistling sounds in strong winds, serving both as an early warning system and a psychological deterrent.

Daily life in Webweave Citadel revolves around the strict schedules of military training, but with a distinctive cultural flair. Soldiers train in web-walking techniques using silk-wood hybrid equipment, while crafters experiment with new ways to incorporate spider-silk engineering into defensive structures. Commander Varis Threadweaver leads the garrison with an emphasis on adapting traditional dwarven combat to make full use of vertical space.

The citadel maintains close ties with nearby Silkstrand Harbor, providing protection in exchange for supplies. Its unique architecture has drawn attention from other coastal settlements, leading to a steady stream of visiting military engineers seeking to study its innovations. Plans are underway to expand the underground portions of the fortress, incorporating natural cave systems into the defensive network.

- Create a vivid, memorable image

- Show practical functionality
- Suggest story possibilities
- Maintain internal consistency
- Reflect local environment

- Include specific architectural details
- Show how daily life functions
