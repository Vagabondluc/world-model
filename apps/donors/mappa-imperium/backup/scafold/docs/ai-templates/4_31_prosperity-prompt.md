# Faction Prosperity Development Prompt

## Required Player Input
- Your Faction's:
  - Race and Core Theme
  - Symbol and Colors
  - Notable Settlements
  - Key Heroes (if established)
- Rolled/Chosen Profession/Industry (from 4.3 table)

## Development Questions

### Core Industry Integration
1. How does your specialty reflect your faction's:
   - Race's natural aptitudes?
   - Cultural symbols and values?
   - Geographic location?
   - Relationship with neighbors?

2. Production Methods:
   - What makes your approach unique?
   - How have you adapted traditional methods?
   - What innovation has your faction introduced?
   - How do your heroes influence the process?

### Settlement Impact
For each major settlement:
- How does it contribute to this industry?
- What unique sub-specialty has it developed?
- What facilities or institutions exist there?
- How has the industry shaped its growth?

### Trade and Relations
1. Primary Markets:
   - Who are your main customers?
   - What do you trade for?
   - How do you protect trade routes?
   - What unique trading practices have you developed?

2. Knowledge Management:
   - How is expertise passed down?
   - Where is training conducted?
   - Who guards trade secrets?
   - What rituals or ceremonies are involved?

## Example Request Format:
```
Faction: Mountain Dwarves
Theme: Crystal and Storm
Settlements: Thunderpeak (capital), Gemflow Valley
Heroes: Master Sculptor, Beast Tamer
Rolled Industry: Magic Training
```

## Example Response:
"Your faction has developed Crystal Resonance Training...

1. Integration Methods:
   - Dwarven crystalwork combines with storm energies
   - Thunder-charged gems serve as teaching tools
   - Each student crafts their own crystal focus
   
2. Settlement Specialties:
   Thunderpeak:
   - The Resonance Academy: hewn from living crystal
   - Training chambers at different altitudes for varied energy levels
   
   Gemflow Valley:
   - Focus on gem-cutting techniques for apprentices
   - Experimental gardens where crystal and storm magic merge

3. Trade Relations:
   - Supply specially tuned training crystals to other factions
   - Exchange students with storm giant academies
   - Protected trade routes marked by crystal beacons

4. Unique Innovations:
   - Crystal harmonic teaching methods
   - Storm energy storage in geometric arrays
   - Thunderwave communication network

[Continue with specific details...]"

## Response Requirements:
1. All elements should:
   - Connect to established faction traits
   - Name specific locations and practices
   - Include concrete details and methods
   - Suggest future development possibilities
   - Create trade/diplomatic opportunities

2. Each specialty should have:
   - Unique production methods
   - Specific training locations
   - Named master practitioners
   - Distinctive tools or techniques
   - Cultural integration elements

3. Avoid:
   - Generic fantasy tropes
   - Disconnected elements
   - Vague descriptions
   - Existing fiction references

## Remember to:
- Use original names for techniques and locations
- Provide granular details about processes
- Connect to existing faction elements
- Suggest inter-settlement dynamics
- Create opportunities for future development
