# Final Era Development Prompt

## Iconic Landmark

"Choose a location that played a pivotal role in your empire's story. Develop it using this format:"

### Historical Significance
- What was this place originally?
- What key event happened here?
- Who were the principal figures involved?
- How did this change your empire?

### Physical Description
- What natural features mark this place?
- What was built or destroyed here?
- What remains visible today?
- What subtle signs hint at its past?

### Current Impact
- How do people interact with this place now?
- What legends surround it?
- What power does it hold?
- What role does it serve?

## World-Ending Omen (Roll 2d6)

"Describe your rolled omen using this format:"

### Initial Manifestation
- Where does it first appear?
- Who first notices it?
- What makes it unmistakable?
- How does it spread?

### Immediate Effect
- What changes instantly?
- Who is most affected?
- What breaks or transforms?
- How do people react?

### Greater Meaning
- What does this herald?
- Why this particular sign?
- What prophecies speak of this?
- How does it reflect your empire's story?

## Required Elements
For both developments:
- Name all significant people and places
- Provide specific, measurable details
- Show clear cause-and-effect relationships
- Connect to your empire's established narrative

Remember: Each entry should stand as a clear, complete record for future storytellers.
