### Core Identity
1. Name:
2. Primary Domain:
3. Secondary Domains (if any):
4. Sacred Symbol:
5. Defining Characteristics:

   - Greatest virtue

### Divine Nature
1. Manifestation:
   - How does this deity appear when interacting with mortals?
   - What natural phenomena are associated with their presence?

### Sacred Sites
For each sacred site associated with the deity:

1. Physical Description:

2. Historical Significance:

   - Event 1: [Cause] led to [Consequence]

3. Current Role:
   - How is the site used today?

### Additional Notes
- What conflicts or tensions exist around this deity?
- How do they influence the mortal world?
- What unique artifacts or creatures are associated with them?

## Response Guidelines:
1. Be specific and detailed in descriptions
2. Focus on cause-and-effect relationships
3. Include elements that can drive narratives
4. Provide concrete examples rather than vague statements
5. Keep cult/worship details minimal unless specifically requested
6. Ensure each deity feels distinct and has clear motivations
