## Requirements for All Events
1. Provide specific names for:
   - All new characters
   - New locations
   - Organizations
   - Cultural practices

2. Include precise details about:
   - Timing
   - Resources
   - Population
   - Territory

3. Show clear causality:
   - Immediate triggers
   - Chain reactions
   - Long-term effects
   - Cultural impacts

4. Maintain consistency with:
   - Previous events
   - Existing structures
   - Established relationships
   - Cultural patterns
