# Minor Faction Narrative Development Prompt

"Your minor faction exists within a web of relationships, history, and ambitions. Using your faction type and rolled event, develop their story through rich detail and clear cause-and-effect relationships."

## Initial Context
Tell us about your faction's:
- Type (from the table)
- Current event (rolled)
- Location relative to major powers
- Recent significant history

## Narrative Development Questions

### Origins and Identity
"What defining moment shaped your faction into what it is today? Consider how this affected their:
- Choice of location
- Methods of survival
- Relations with others
- Core beliefs or drives"

### Power Structure
"Who leads your faction and how did they rise to power? Tell us about:
- The circumstances of their emergence
- How they maintain authority
- Their vision for the future
- Those who support or oppose them"

### Current Event Development
"How does this new event flow from and change your faction's story? Detail:
- The direct catalyst
- Key figures involved
- Immediate reactions
- Rippling consequences"

### Environmental Impact
"How does your faction change their surroundings? Describe:
- Physical alterations to the landscape
- Effects on local resources
- Influence on nearby communities
- Signs of their presence"

## Narrative Focuses by Faction Type

### For Magic Users
"How does magic manifest in your society? Tell us about:
- A ritual gone wrong that changed your practices
- The price your power demands
- How magic shapes daily life
- What outsiders fear most about your abilities"

### For Hives
"How does your collective function and grow? Describe:
- The pattern of your expansion
- Your method of communication
- What drives your decisions
- How you transform conquered territory"

### For Tribes/Bandits/Pirates
"What traditions define your people? Detail:
- A pivotal raid or battle that changed your methods
- How leadership changes hands
- What marks your territory as yours
- Why others fear or respect you"

### For Cults/Lairs
"What dark truths drive your devotion? Chronicle:
- The revelation that founded your belief
- How you select new initiates
- What your rituals have discovered
- How your influence spreads"

### For Monsters
"What makes your presence legendary? Tell us about:
- The first time your power was demonstrated
- How you've adapted to resistance
- What marks your territory
- Why lesser beings serve you"

## Narrative Guidelines

### Rich Detail Development
"Every new element should have:
- A clear origin
- Specific characteristics
- Notable impact
- Future implications"

### Naming Principles
"When naming new elements, consider:
- How names reflect history
- What they reveal about culture
- How they connect to purpose
- What they suggest about relationships"

### Causality Guidelines
"For each development:
- Show clear triggers
- Detail immediate effects
- Explore unintended consequences
- Suggest future implications"

### Cultural Integration
"Demonstrate how changes affect:
- Daily practices
- Power structures
- Resource management
- Outside relationships"

## Remember
- Every name should be original and meaningful
- Each detail should serve the larger narrative
- All changes should have clear causes and effects
- Development should feel organic and connected

"How does this event write the next chapter in your faction's story?"
