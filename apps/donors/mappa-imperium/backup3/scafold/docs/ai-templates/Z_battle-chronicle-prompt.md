# Battle Chronicle Template

"Record this conflict in your empire's history, detailing its course and consequences."

## Required Context
- Attacking faction
- Defending settlement
- Current season/conditions
- Strategic importance
- Prior relationships

## Chronicle Format

### [Battle Name], [Season/Year]
Opening statement establishing the conflict's scale and stakes.

### The Field of Battle
One paragraph describing:
- Battle location
- Key terrain features
- Weather conditions
- Initial positions
- Strategic advantages

### Forces Engaged
One paragraph detailing:
- Commanders present
- Notable units
- Special weapons/tactics
- Morale and readiness
- Key advantages/disadvantages

### The Battle Unfolds
Based on roll result (1d6):

**1. Devastating Loss and Counterattack**
- Initial assault details
- Turning point moment
- Defender's rally
- Pursuit and counter-strike
- Monument raised
- Hero's emergence

**2. Failed Attack**
- Initial approach
- Defense preparations
- Critical moment
- Enemy withdrawal
- Aftermath
- Lessons learned

**3. Stalemate**
- Opening maneuvers
- Grinding engagement
- Mounting losses
- Eventual withdrawal
- Cost to both sides
- Strategic implications

**4. Successful Assault**
- Breaking point
- Key tactical success
- Settlement's fall
- Immediate changes
- New governance
- Population impact

**5. Great Victory**
- Overwhelming force
- Defense collapse
- Complete conquest
- Fort construction
- Territory reorganization
- Power shift

**6. Great Campaign**
- Initial victory
- Strategic momentum
- Second target
- Complete success
- Hero's rise
- Monument creation

### Aftermath Section
One paragraph covering:
- Immediate consequences
- Casualties and captures
- Territory changes
- Power shifts
- Resource impacts

### Legacy Section
One paragraph describing:
- Long-term effects
- Cultural changes
- New traditions
- Lasting monuments
- Future implications

## Required Elements

### For All Chronicles:
- Name all commanders and heroes
- Specify troop numbers/types
- Detail tactical decisions
- Show clear cause-effect
- Name significant locations
- Include specific dates/times

### Based on Result:
- Detail monuments raised
- Name emerging heroes
- Specify territorial changes
- Document power shifts
- Record resource impacts

Remember: This battle should feel like a pivotal moment in your empire's history, with clear causes and lasting consequences.
