# Event Form Spec: Gifted Academy (15)

## Purpose
To create a new `Settlement` card for a specialized academy or institution.

## AI Integration
- **Primary Prompt**: `docs/ai-templates/5_1_empire-events-prompts.md` (Event 15)
- **Context**: Player's faction, the academy's specialty, and its founding principles.
- **Output**: A narrative describing the founding of the academy, its teaching methods, and its cultural influence.

## Form Fields

### 1. The Institution
- **Academy Name**
  - **Label**: Name of the Academy/Sanctuary/Library
  - **Type**: Text
  - **Required**: Yes
- **Specialty Focus**
  - **Label**: What is the specialty of this institution?
  - **Type**: Dropdown
  - **Required**: Yes
  - **Options**: Spellcasting, Research & Sciences, Monastic Martial Arts, Druidic Arts, History & Lore, Fine Arts, Military Strategy.
- **Location**
  - **Label**: Where is the academy located?
  - **Type**: Text
  - **Required**: Yes
- **Founding Scholars**
  - **Label**: Name the founding scholars or masters
  - **Type**: Text
  - **Required**: No

### 2. Academy Details
- **Teaching Methods**
  - **Label**: Describe their unique teaching methods
  - **Type**: Textarea
  - **Required**: No
- **Facility Design**
  - **Label**: Describe the design of the facility
  - **Type**: Textarea
  - **Required**: No
- **Student Selection**
  - **Label**: How are students selected?
  - **Type**: Textarea
  - **Required**: No

### 3. Cultural Impact
- **Knowledge Impact**
  - **Label**: How does the knowledge from this academy impact the empire?
  - **Type**: Textarea
  - **Required**: No
- **Cultural Influence**
  - **Label**: What is its cultural influence?
  - **Type**: Textarea
  - **Required**: No
- **Innovation Effects**
  - **Label**: What innovations has it produced?
  - **Type**: Textarea
  - **Required**: No
