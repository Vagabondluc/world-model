# Event Form Spec: War! (10)

## Purpose
To initiate a conflict between the player's faction and another settlement, creating a `War` card. This is functionally identical to the Era V event.

## AI Integration
- **Primary Prompt**: `docs/ai-templates/Z_battle-chronicle-prompt.md`
- **Context**: Details from both attacker and defender.
- **Output**: A detailed battle chronicle.

## Form Fields

### 1. War Declaration
- **Target Settlement**
  - **Label**: Select a settlement to attack
  - **Type**: Dropdown
  - **Required**: Yes
  - **Options**: (Populated with all non-player-owned settlements)
- **Casus Belli**
  - **Label**: Justification for War
  - **Type**: Textarea
  - **Required**: Yes

### 2. Attacker's Strategy (Your Faction)
- **Military Commander**
  - **Label**: Who is leading your army?
  - **Type**: Text
  - **Required**: Yes
- **Army Composition**
  - **Label**: Describe your attacking force
  - **Type**: Textarea
  - **Required**: Yes

### 3. Battle Resolution
- **Roll for Outcome**
  - **Label**: Battle Outcome (1d6)
  - **Type**: Dice Roller (Button)
  - **Required**: Yes
  - **Help Text**: Roll on the War! table (pg 16).
