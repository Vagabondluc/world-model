Minor Faction

1d6EventDescription
1ExpandAdd a settlement
2ColonizeBuild a new coastal settlement
3MilitarizeDraw in a new fort or military settlement

5Epic ConstructionA new academy, altar, tower? Draw in a new construction
6ExpandConnect a road to a neighbor and build a trade settlement on that road. Or, create a new coastal trading port.
