# Era VI: Age of Collapse Narrative Prompt

"Your empire faces its twilight years. Each event now writes a chapter in its transformation or decline. Tell the story of how this moment reshapes your realm."

## Narrative Foundation
Begin with your empire's:
- Current state of power
- Key vulnerabilities
- Remaining strengths
- Most valuable resources
- Critical relationships

## Event Categories & Narrative Approaches

### Supernatural Upheaval (3, 4, 17)
"Something beyond mortal understanding emerges. Tell us:

How does it first manifest? Describe the initial signs - strange lights, unsettling sounds, inexplicable phenomena. What do common people first notice? How do the wise interpret these omens?

What immediate impact does it have on the land and people? Paint a picture of the transformation - physical changes, social reactions, power dynamics. How do different segments of society respond?

Who attempts to understand or control it? Introduce key figures who rise to prominence during this crisis. What price do they pay for their involvement?

How does this change your empire's relationship with the supernatural? Detail the new normal that emerges in its wake."

### Political Transformation (5, 15, 16)
"Power structures shift dramatically. Chronicle:

What breaks the old order? Describe the catalyst - a decision, a revelation, a betrayal. Who stands to gain or lose the most?

How do different factions respond? Show us the complex web of alliances and betrayals that emerges. What ancient loyalties shatter? What unlikely partnerships form?

What new order attempts to rise? Detail its structure, its promises, its compromises. How does it address the failures of the old system?

What lasting scars remain on the society? Explore the deeper changes in social fabric and cultural identity."

### Environmental Catastrophe (12, 13, 18)
"The land itself turns against civilization. Describe:

How does the catastrophe unfold? Paint a vivid picture of its progression - the first signs, the mounting crisis, the point of no return. What makes this disaster particularly devastating to your empire?

What crucial resources or locations are lost? Detail the specific impacts on your empire's foundation of power. Which losses hit hardest?

How do survivors adapt? Show us the desperate innovations, the abandoned traditions, the new ways of living that emerge. What knowledge proves most valuable?

What remains as testament to what was lost? Describe the physical and cultural scars that will mark this region for generations."

### Social Upheaval (6, 7, 14)
"Society transforms under pressure. Tell us:

What drives people to abandon old ways? Explore the complex pressures - fear, necessity, new beliefs. What makes the old ways untenable?

Who emerges as leaders in this transformation? Show us those who give voice to the changes, who guide people through them. What gives them authority?

How do communities reorganize? Detail the new structures that emerge - physical, social, spiritual. What holds people together now?

What aspects of the old culture survive, and how do they change? Trace the evolution of crucial traditions and beliefs."

### Military Conflicts (8, 9, 10)
"War reshapes the realm. Chronicle:

What makes this conflict different from previous ones? Describe the unique pressures, tactics, or stakes involved. Why do traditional responses fail?

How does the war transform your military? Show us the adaptation of strategies, the cost of victory or defeat. What sacrifices are demanded?

What unexpected consequences emerge? Detail the ripple effects through society, economy, and culture. What opportunities arise from chaos?

How does this change your empire's relationship with power? Explore the new understanding that emerges from this trial."

## Narrative Elements to Include

### People
For every new figure:
- Their rise to prominence
- Their unique perspective
- Their lasting impact
- Their ultimate fate

### Places
For each affected location:
- Physical transformation
- New significance
- Lost elements
- Future implications

### Society
Show changes in:
- Power structures
- Daily life
- Beliefs and values
- Relationships between groups

### Legacy
Detail how this event:
- Changes empire identity
- Affects future generations
- Transforms relationships
- Creates new possibilities

## Writing Guidelines

Remember to:
- Name every significant person, place, and development with original, meaningful names
- Provide specific, granular details about how changes occur
- Show clear cause-and-effect relationships in all developments
- Connect each event to your empire's larger narrative
- Leave traces that future generations will remember

"This is not just decline - it is transformation. How does your empire change in response to this crisis?"
