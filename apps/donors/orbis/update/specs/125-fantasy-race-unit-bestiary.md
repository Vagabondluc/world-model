## Unit Classification System

### By Role
- **Worker**: Resource gathering and construction
- **Military**: Combat and defense
- **Magical**: Spellcasting and support

### By Size
- **Tiny**: Sprite, Mote, Droplet
- **Small**: Goblin, Imp, Faerie
- **Medium**: Human, Elf, Dwarf
- **Large**: Orc, Centaur, Werewolf
- **Huge**: Giant, Dragon, Colossus

### By Terrain
- **Terrestrial**: Land-based units
- **Aquatic**: Water-based units
- **Aerial**: Flying units
- **Underground**: Subterranean units
- **Ethereal**: Non-material units
